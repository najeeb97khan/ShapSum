[1] BFN [Text] Beijing, June 26 (XINHUA) -- Floods have all receded along Xijiang, Beijiang, Xiangjiang and Ganjiang except at a section of the Xijiang River in Wuzhou city, Guangxi, a spokesman from the State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters announced today. [2] The spokesman attributed the falling water level to the major water conservancy projects which help to mitigate losses.
