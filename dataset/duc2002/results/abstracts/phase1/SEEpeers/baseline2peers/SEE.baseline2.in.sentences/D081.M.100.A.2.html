[1] BFN [Text] Donetsk Oblast [no date as received] -- As UNIAN reported, miners at the "Ordzhonikidzevuhillya" coal production association and the "Yenakiyevo" and "Poltava" pits went on strike in Yenakiyevo on 27 June, demanding payment of wage arrears. [2] The strike continued at these mines on 28 June. [3] Some of the miners at the Karl Marx, "Chernovonyy Zhovten," "Chervonyy Profinter," and "Yunkom" pits joined the strike. [4] A total of 1,200 of the association's 2,248 workers are on strike. [5] A rally consisting of 350 miners was held on 28 June near the town council building where demands were made to pay the wages and dismiss the director general of the "Ordzhonikidzevuhillya" production association.
