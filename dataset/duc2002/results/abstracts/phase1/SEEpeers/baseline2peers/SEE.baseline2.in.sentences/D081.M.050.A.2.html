[1] BFN [Text] Donetsk Oblast [no date as received] -- As UNIAN reported, miners at the "Ordzhonikidzevuhillya" coal production association and the "Yenakiyevo" and "Poltava" pits went on strike in Yenakiyevo on 27 June, demanding payment of wage arrears. [2] The strike continued at these mines on 28 June. [3] Some of the miners at the Karl Marx, "Chernovonyy Zhovten," "Chervonyy Profinter," and "Yunkom" pits joined the strike.
