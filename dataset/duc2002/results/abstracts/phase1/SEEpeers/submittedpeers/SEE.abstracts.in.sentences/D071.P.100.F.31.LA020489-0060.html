[1] More than 2,500 dogs, representing 138 breeds, will compete for prizes at Sunday's All Breed Dog Show and Obedience Trials at the Los Angeles Sports Arena. [2] Other attractions include the appearance of Warren Eckstein, radio host of "Pet Show," who will answer behavioral questions about dogs (as well as cats, pigs and goldfish, if he's asked); free veterinary advice; a demonstration of how 4-H youngsters help train puppies to become guide dogs, and more than 70 vendors selling dog paraphernalia. [3] A computer is provided for visitors' use to find out which breeds would best suit their life styles. [4] Show hours are 8 a.m. to 9 p.m. [5] Admission: adults, $4.50; children, $1.
