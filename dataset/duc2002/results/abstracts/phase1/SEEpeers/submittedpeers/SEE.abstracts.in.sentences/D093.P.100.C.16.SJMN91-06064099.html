[1] A boat carrying hundreds of Somali refugees struck a reef off Kenya and sank, killing more than 130 people, newspapers reported. [2] The accident involving a vessel ferrying about 600 people occurred Friday near Malindi, a tourist resort about 400 miles southeast of Nairobi. First accounts of the accident were reported Sunday in Kenyan newspapers. [3] The boat with the refugees had set sail from the Somali town of Kismayo, 250 miles north of Malindi, on Feb. 26. [4] Eleven Somalis drowned when a boat capsized off Kenya near Lamu, north of Malindi, on Feb. 1 .
