[1] Iraq's powerful army claimed victory today in its lightning invasion of this small, oil-rich kingdom. [2] Tank-led troops quickly seized the ruler's palace and government buildings, and the emir fled to Saudi Arabia, Kuwaiti officials said. Baghdad television said Iraq could withdraw its forces within days or weeks, depending on how quickly the situation stabilized . The assault followed two weeks of tension between Kuwait and Iraq, caused by Iraqi accusations that Kuwait stole oil from a disputed border area.
