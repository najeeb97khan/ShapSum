[1] BFN [Text] Comrade Wen Jiabao [member of CPC Central Committee Secretariat] and his party left Changsha for Zhuzhou and Xiangtan yesterday. [2] He inspected the flooded areas, including Zhuzhou City's Xiawan dike, the Hexi New and High Technology Development Zone, and Linghuayuan and Xiangtan's Yangtianhu, Xingxiadi, Zhoujiangdi, and Shiwandalou, where rescue and relief work was in full swing. [3] After listening to reports submitted by the two cities on their efforts to fight the flooding and do relief work, he issued important instructions.
