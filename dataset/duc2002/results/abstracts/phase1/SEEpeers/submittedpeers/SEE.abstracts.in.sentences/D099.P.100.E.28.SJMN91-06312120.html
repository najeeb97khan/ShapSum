[1] TO MOST PEOPLE, marathons and vacations are mutually exclusive. [2] Vacation, according to Webster, means "freedom from any activity; rest; respite". [3] A marathon, says the same source, is "a footrace of 26 miles, 385 yards, run over an open course" To help these folks along, companies arrange tours for marathon vacations. [4] "Skiers have mountain ranges, tennis players have resorts, why can't a runner take a marathon vacation"? [5] In addition to air fare and hotel, runners are guaranteed spots in restricted entry races such as the London Marathon. [6] Some marathons, like Portland's, include additional races: a five-mile run, a five-mile walk and a kids run.
