[1] THE STORY OF KELLEY [2] A two-time winner in Boston (1935 and 1945) and runner-up seven times, Mr. Kelley is to be the oldest competitor in the race in 1991 -- as he has been for 15 years. [3] Though Tomomitsu isn't the oldest person ever to enter the Boston Marathon, Mr. Kelley is the oldest to run it. [4] Recalls Suni Tomomitsu, one of the runners: "At first we thought, `Oh, wow, he's like Mafia man'. [5] Then we said, `Oh no, this is nice Johnny Kelley'". [6] Mr. Kelley aims to run till he is at least 100 years old.
