[1] THE STORY OF KOHL [2] With the surge of the East German Social Democrats, political analysts are pointing to the strange role they could play in the ultimate political fate of Kohl. Ever since reunification became a blazing issue, Kohl's standing in West German opinion polls has risen, and if the national elections, now scheduled for December, were held on Feb. 12, 1990, he would have a good chance of leading his Christian Democratic Union to victory . West German Chancellor Helmut Kohl declared on his return from Moscow on Feb. 10, 1990 that "the way is now free" for German reunification .
