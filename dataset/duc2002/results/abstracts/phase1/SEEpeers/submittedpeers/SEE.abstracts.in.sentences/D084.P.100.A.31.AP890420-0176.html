[1] The bodies of 47 sailors killed in the gun explosion on the Iowa arrived in the United States on Thursday as the Navy tried to figure out what caused the battleship's turret to blow up. [2] Navy Secretary William Ball attended a memorial service at the Delaware base, home of the armed services' largest mortuary. [3] Eleven men escaped from powder magazines deep within the ship at the base of the turret.
