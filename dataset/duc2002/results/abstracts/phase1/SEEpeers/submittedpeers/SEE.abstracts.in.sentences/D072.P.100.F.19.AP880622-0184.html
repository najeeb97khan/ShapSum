[1] Beverly Sills, Lauren Bacall, Betty Comden and Phyllis Newman are among performers who will sing, act and make guest appearances at a birthday bash in August for conductor Leonard Bernstein. [2] The Leonard Bernstein Gala Birthday Performance is a benefit concert scheduled for the composer's 70th birthday, 08/25/1988, to raise money for the Tanglewood Music Center, where Bernstein got his conducting start.
