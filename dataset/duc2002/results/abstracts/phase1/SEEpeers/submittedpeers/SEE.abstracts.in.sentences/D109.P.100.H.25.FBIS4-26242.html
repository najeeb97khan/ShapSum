[1] FLOOD AFFECTS CHANGSHA, XIANGJIANG RIVER AND ZHUZHOU [2] This capital of central China's Hunan province has been hit by floods in the wake of a succession of rainstorms on the upper and middle reaches of the Xiangjiang River. [3] The city government of Changsha has asked residents within the endangered areas to relocate themselves and remove their property to safer places, in case of the occurrence of more serious flooding. [4] The flood waters had reached the boulevard along the Xiangjiang River, Xiahe Street and the western part of Zhongshan Street in Changsha proper.
