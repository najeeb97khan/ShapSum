[1] Rains from Hurricane Gilbert sent a river raging over its banks in Monterrey, sweeping at least 10 policemen to their deaths and overturning buses loaded with evacuees from Matamoros, police said today. [2] The flooding occurred in Monterrey, an industrial city of 3 million people, located 110 miles from the Texas border and 140 miles inland from Matamoros. [3] Several buses loaded with evacuees overturned in the raging water and more were feared drowned. [4] About 5,000 people were evacuated inland. [5] ``Up to now the damages are not considered very grave,'' said Jose Inez Gonzalez, a Matamoros government spokesman.
