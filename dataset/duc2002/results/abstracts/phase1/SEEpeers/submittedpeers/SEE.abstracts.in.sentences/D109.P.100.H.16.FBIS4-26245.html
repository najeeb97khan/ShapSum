[1] Information from the State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters revealed that the flood peak on the Xijiang River today reached Guangdong Province. [2] By eight o'clock yesterday morning, a peak flood water level of 25.91 m [meters] was recorded at the flood monitoring station of Guangxi's Wuzhou city, 10.91 m above the danger level. [3] The flood peak on the Beijiang River in Guangdong Province passed through Qingyuan city by 23:00 hours last night, and the water level was recorded as 14.72 m, about 4.22 m above the danger mark; the speed was 17,400 cu m per second.
