[1] Port Workers, Coal Miners Join Strike To Legalize Solidarity Port workers in Szczecin and southwestern coal miners today declared sympathy strikes with miners demanding the legalization of Solidarity, opposition activists said. [2] In support of an estimated 3,000 colleagues striking for a second day at the July Manifesto mine in Jastrzebie, workers at the nearby Morcinek mine refused today to take up their tools and launched their own occupation strike. [3] Andrzej Andrzejczak, head of the Morcinek mine strike committee, said: ``We support all of the demands of the July Manifesto mine workers''.
