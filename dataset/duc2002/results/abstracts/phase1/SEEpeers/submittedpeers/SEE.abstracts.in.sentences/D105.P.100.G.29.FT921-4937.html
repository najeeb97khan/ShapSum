[1] A CEASEFIRE in Moldova was threatened yesterday when a truck driver transporting militiamen was shot dead by machinegun fire in the self-proclaimed Trans Dnestr republic, home of the Russian-speaking minority. [2] The shooting came shortly after the ceasefire came into effect between the Moldovan authorities, dominated by ethnic Romanians, and the minority based in the republic on the east bank of the river Dnestr. [3] At least nine people have been killed in clashes over the past week. [4] As in the more severe conflict in Nagorno-Karabakh, the underlying tensions are growing and neither side believes it is losing.
