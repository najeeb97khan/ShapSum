[1] April 18 _ Thousands of students march in Beijing and Shanghai, expressing dissatisfaction with the government and shouting "Long live Hu Yaobang! May 13 _ About 2,000 students begin a hunger strike in Tiananmen Square. [2] May 14 _ Thousands flock to the square to back the students. [3] May 15 _ The government deadline for students to leave the square passes. [4] May 16 _ Hundreds of thousands occupy the square. [5] Journalists and intellectuals join the protest. [6] The students later decide to end the hunger strike. [7] Students resume their hunger strike, then abandon it, saying they will need their strength for the struggle ahead.
