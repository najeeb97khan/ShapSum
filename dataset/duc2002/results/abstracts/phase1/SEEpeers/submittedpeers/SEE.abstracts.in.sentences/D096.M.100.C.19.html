[1] In a dramatic finish, the San Francisco 49ers football team won the Super Bowl on Sunday, 01/21/1989, with a 20-16 victory over the Cincinnati Bengals. [2] President Bush congratulated the San Francisco 49ers on Friday, 02/03/1989, and said they ``rewrote the record books'' with their Super Bowl victory. [3] The San Francisco 49ers' 55-10 rout of the Denver Broncos was the lowest-rated Super Bowl in 21 years and the third-lowest ever. [4] The highest-rated Super Bowl was in 1982, when the 49ers' 26-21 victory over Cincinnati got a 49.1 on CBS.
