[1] HURRICANE AFFECTS GULF OF MEXICO, FLORIDA AND MIAMI [2] US CITIES along the Gulf of Mexico from Alabama to eastern Texas were on storm watch last night as Hurricane Andrew headed west after sweeping across southern Florida, causing at least eight deaths and severe property damage.
