[1] Highlights of Lucille Ball's career: 1911: Born 08/06/1911, in Jamestown N.Y. 1933: Goes to Hollywood as movie showgirl for RKO, later becoming a successful actress appearing in more than 75 films. [2] 1940: Marries Desi Arnaz. [3] 1951: They form Desilu Productions with $5,000 and "I Love Lucy" begins its historic six-year domination of television comedy. [4] 1957: Desilu purchases RKO studios and begins producing other programs. [5] 1960: The Arnazes divorce, with Desi selling his share of the studio to Lucy for $3 million. [6] 1961: Miss Ball marries night-club comic Gary Morton, who becomes her executive producer.
