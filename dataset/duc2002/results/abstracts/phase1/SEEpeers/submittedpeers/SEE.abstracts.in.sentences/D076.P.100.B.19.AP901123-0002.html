[1] For almost a decade, Margaret Thatcher was regarded at the White House as America 's best friend, the trusted ally whose strong views reinforced the conservative tone of administration policies. [2] Among Western allies, she was alone at Ronald Reagan 's side in 1986 in supporting the U.S. bombing of Libya. [3] In the Persian Gulf crisis, she boldly joined with George Bush in sending troops to the Middle East. [4] Mrs. Thatcher encouraged the huge Pentagon buildup that Reagan nurtured under the theme of peace through strength.
