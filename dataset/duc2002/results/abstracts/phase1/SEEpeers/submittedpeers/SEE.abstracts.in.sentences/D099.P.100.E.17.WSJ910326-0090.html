[1] John Adelbert Kelley is a throwback to a time when marathoners did it for glory and cab fare. [2] Mr. Kelley is in training for the Boston Marathon -- his 60th Boston Marathon. [3] Mr. Kelley is such an institution that Hopkinton, Mass., where the race begins, has made him an honorary citizen. [4] Though he isn't the oldest person ever to enter the Boston Marathon, Mr. Kelley is the oldest to run it. [5] The rosy-cheeked, supple Mr. Kelley is a medical wonder. [6] Unlike many runners, Mr. Kelley doesn't bother with stretching exercises before he runs in the morning. [7] Mr. Kelley aims to run till he is at least 100 years old.
