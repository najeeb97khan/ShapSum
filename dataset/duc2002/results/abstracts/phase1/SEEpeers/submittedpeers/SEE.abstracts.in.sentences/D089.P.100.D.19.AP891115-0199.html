[1] Thunderstorms wreaked havoc across the middle Mississippi Valley and the Southeast on Wednesday with lethal tornadoes, wind up to 100 mph and hail up to the size of baseballs. [2] The thunderstorms developed along and ahead of a strong cold front and swept eastward from Missouri and Arkansas. [3] Severe thunderstorms also developed over Alabama and South Carolina during the afternoon, and the weather service reported a tornado north of Greenwood, S.C. [4] Other showers and thunderstorms, with locally heavy rain, were widely scattered along the northern Atlantic Coast.
