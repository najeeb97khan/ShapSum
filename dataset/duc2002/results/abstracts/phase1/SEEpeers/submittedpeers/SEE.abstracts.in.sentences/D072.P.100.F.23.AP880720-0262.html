[1] Actresses Lauren Bacall, Betty Comden and Phyllis Newman are among performers in the birthday bash being thrown in August for conductor Leonard Bernstein at the music center where he got his start. [2] The Tanglewood Music Center also is celebrating the 50th anniversary of its Music Shed this summer with a special concert by the resident Boston Symphony Orchestra featuring the same music, Beethoven's Ninth Symphony, that inaugurated the building in 1938. [3] The Bernstein weekend alone, with tickets as high as $5,000 each, may bring in more than $1 million for the festival's endowment fund, according to Caroline Smedvig of the symphony.
