[1] Here is a chronology of Hurricane Hugo's destructive path across the Caribbean into South Carolina: Sept. 11 _ Forecasters monitor new tropical wave forming off West Africa, southwest of the Cape Verde Islands. [2] Sept. 12 _ Storm reaches the 39 mph tropical storm threshold and is named Hugo. [3] Sept. 13 _ Hugo develops into a hurricane as winds reach 74 mph. [4] Sept. 16 _ Hugo bears down on Guadeloupe and Dominica with winds reaching 140 mph. [5] Five people are reported killed, 84 injured and more than 15,000 homeless on the island of 340,000 people. [6] At least one person is reported killed on St. Croix.
