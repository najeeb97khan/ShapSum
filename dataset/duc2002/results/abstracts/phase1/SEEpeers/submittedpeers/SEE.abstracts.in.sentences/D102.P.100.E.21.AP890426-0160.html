[1] Lucille Ball was mourned Wednesday by the western New York hometown she left for Hollywood more than half a century ago. [2] The 77-year-old comedian died of a ruptured aorta, the main heart artery, at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center in Los Angeles, where she was recovering from emergency heart surgery last week. [3] Miss Ball was born Aug. 6, 1911, in this city of 40,000 people about 60 miles south of Buffalo, the daughter of an electrician and a concert pianist. [4] Although the comedian hadn't visited Jamestown since 1956 and had no family there, she regularly donated money to charities, civic events and Jamestown Community College, said Mayor Steven Carlson.
