[1] Unidentified assailants hurled a grenade and fired shots today at the home of relatives of Nelson Mandela, killing a baby girl and injuring her parents, police said. [2] ANC officials said the dead child was the great niece of Mandela's father, making her a distant cousin of Nelson Mandela. [3] An ANC statement said, ``The ANC notes the curious coincidence of an attack on a man bearing the Mandela name at this time''. [4] Given the distant connection between Mandela and the family, police said they did not know if the attack was deliberate or just a random attack linked to the general violence.
