[1] Strikes have shut down more than 200 mines and factories in the country's two top coal-producing regions. [2] Miners began walking out 07/11/1989 in Siberia's Kuznetsk Basin coal region. [3] On 07/15/1989, miners in the Donetsk coal basin began walking off the job. [4] The miners are demanding more local control over their industry. [5] Miners are demanding a 40 percent wage increase for night work, improved living conditions, a fixed day off and retirement after 20 years of underground work.
