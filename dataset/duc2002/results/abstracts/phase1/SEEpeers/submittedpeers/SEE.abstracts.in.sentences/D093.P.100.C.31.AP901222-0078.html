[1] Chief Petty Officer Lance Vickery recalls waves tumbling into the stern of the bobbing ferry headed back to the USS Saratoga and joking, ``Whoa, looks like we're flooding''. [2] As Vickery was being treated for bleeding lungs caused by water inhalation, his hospital roommate, Seaman Michael Benjamin, 19, of San Antonio, Texas, was recovering from a neck injury suffered when a rescue boat hit him while he was in the water. [3] Dazed, Benjamin stripped off his jacket, shirt and shoes and kept treading water until he found a piece of driftwood to cling to.
