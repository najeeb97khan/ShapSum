[1] Jewish Leaders To Meet in Berlin on German Reunification The Berlin villa where the Nazi leadership met to implement the ``final solution'' against Europe's Jewish population will be the site of a World Jewish Congress conference on German reunification. [2] ``The world is expecting a Jewish statement on reunification,'' said Edgar Bronfman, the president of the World Jewish Congress. [3] Some 100 members of the World Jewish Congress from about 70 countries will attend the conference at the villa in Wannsee, Berlin, where on Jan. 20, 1942, high German government and party officials adopted a plan to exterminate the Jews.
