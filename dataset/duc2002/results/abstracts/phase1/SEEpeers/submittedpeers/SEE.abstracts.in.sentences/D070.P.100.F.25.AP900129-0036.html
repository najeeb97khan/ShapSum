[1] THE STORY OF HONECKER [2] Ousted East German leader Erich Honecker, who is expected to be indicted for high treason, was arrested on Jan. 28, 1990 morning upon release from a hospital and taken to prison, the official news agency ADN said. [3] Honecker, the aging hard-line Stalinist leader who ruled East Germany for 18 years until his ouster on Oct. 18, had previously been declared too ill to withstand imprisonment. [4] Earlier in Jan. 1990, East German prosecutors said Honecker and former state security chief Erich Mielke would be charged with treason and corruption charges for misuse of their positions and state funds.
