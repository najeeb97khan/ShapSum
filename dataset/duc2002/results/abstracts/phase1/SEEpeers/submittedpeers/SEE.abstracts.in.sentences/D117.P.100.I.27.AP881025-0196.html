[1] Australian novelist Peter Carey was awarded the coveted Booker Prize for fiction Tuesday night for his love story, ``Oscar and Lucinda''. [2] A panel of five judges unanimously announced the award of the $26,250 prize after an 80-minute deliberation during a banquet at London's ancient Guildhall. [3] The judges made their selection from 102 books published in Britain in the past 12 months and which they read in their homes.
