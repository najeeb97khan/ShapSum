[1] THE STORY OF WEN JIABAO [2] A central work team led Wen Jiabao, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and member of the CPC Central Committee Secretariat, arrived in Jiangxi from Hunan this morning to provide on-the-spot guideance for Jiangxi to conduct flood-fighting and relief work and to resume industrial and agricultural production. [3] Wen Jiabao highly commended Jiangxi's servicemembers and people for their spirit that stresses fostering unity and coordinated efforts, striving to help and rescue themselves, and continually fighting when encountering extraordinarily disastrous floods.
