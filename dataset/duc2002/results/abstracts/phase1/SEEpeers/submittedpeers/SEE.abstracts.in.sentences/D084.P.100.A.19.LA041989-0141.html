[1] A fiery explosion in a giant gun turret rocked the refitted battleship Iowa on maneuvers in the Atlantic on 04/19/1989, killing at least 47 sailors and injuring an undetermined number, the Pentagon said. [2] Defense Department spokesman Dan Howard said the explosion may have resulted from the blast of a 110 - pound gunpowder bag used to fire shells that weigh more than a ton a distance of up to 23 miles. [3] The spokesman said he had no word on the number of injured. [4] The aircraft carrier Coral Sea steamed to the scene, sending out helicopters to land supplies and medical crews on the ship.
