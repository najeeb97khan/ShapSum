[1] Here are highlights of Lucille Ball's career: 1911 _ Born Aug. 6, 1911, in Jamestown N.Y., the daughter of an electrician and concert pianist Desiree Ball. [2] 1940 _ Marries Desi Arnaz, a Cuban percussionist and bandleader when both were making the movie ``Too Many Girls''. [3] 1953 _ ``I Love Lucy'' episode in which Lucy Ricardo gives birth to Little Ricky is telecast the same day that Lucille Ball, in real life, gives birth to second child, Desi Jr. 1957 _ Desilu purchases RKO studios and begins producing other programs. [4] 1968 _ ``The Lucy Show'' is redesigned and becomes ``Here's Lucy''.
