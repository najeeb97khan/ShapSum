[1] MONUMENTS IN TIANANMEN SQUARE, BEIJING, AND CHINA [2] A series of monuments happened in Tiananmen Square, Beijing, China, and other places between June 3, 1989 and June 9, 1989. [3] At 4:30 a.m., armored personnel carriers entered the square and troops began moving slowly toward several thousand students clustered around a monument to revolutionary heroes in the center. [4] Gunfire could be heard from several sides, and tracers zipped through the sky above Mao Tse-tung's mausoleum, one of the main monuments to Chinese communism in the square. [5] The Monument to Revolutionary Martyrs, where the students took a final stand early on June 3, 1989, was swept clean.
