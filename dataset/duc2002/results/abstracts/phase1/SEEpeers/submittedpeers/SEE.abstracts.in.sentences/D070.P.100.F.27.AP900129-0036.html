[1] Ousted East German leader Erich Honecker, who is expected to be indicted for high treason, was arrested Monday morning upon release from a hospital and taken to prison, the official news agency ADN said. [2] The news agency said the 77-year-old Honecker was arrested after being released from East Berlin's Charite hospital, where he had undergone surgery for a malignant kidney tumor. [3] Honecker was immediately taken to East Berlin's Rummelsburg prison, the brief dispatch said. [4] ADN made not mention if Rummelsburg prison was equipped with a medical facility.
