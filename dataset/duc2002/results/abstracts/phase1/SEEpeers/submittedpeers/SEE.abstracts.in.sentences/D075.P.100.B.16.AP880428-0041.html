[1] Study: Three-Day Hospital Stays Enough for Some Heart Attack Victims New treatments that stop a heart attack in its tracks may help victims to get out of the hospital more quickly than ever before, perhaps even three days after their attacks, a study published today concludes. [2] Topol said the early discharges were made possible by two recent medical advances _ tissue plasminogen activator, or TPA, and angioplasty. [3] When given quickly after a heart attack, TPA can dissolve clots in the heart's arteries and minimize damage. [4] Now, doctors typically keep heart attack patients hospitalized seven to 10 days.
