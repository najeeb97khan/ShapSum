[1] HURRICANES IN DOMINICAN REPUBLIC, PUERTO RICO, AND JAMAICA [2] A series of hurricanes happened in Dominican Republic, Puerto Rico, Jamaica, and other places between Sept. 11, 1988 and Sept. 16, 1988. [3] Hurricanes from 1935 and 1969 are also mentioned. [4] Hurricane Gilbert swept toward Jamaica on Sept. 11, 1988 with 100-mile-an-hour winds, and officials issued warnings to residents on the southern coasts of the Dominican Republic, Haiti and Cuba. [5] Heavy rain and stiff winds downed power lines and caused flooding in the Dominican Republic on Sept. 10, 1988 night as the hurricane's center passed just south of the Barahona peninsula, then less than 100 miles from neighboring Haiti.
