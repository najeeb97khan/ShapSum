[1] EARTHQUAKE AFFECTS SAN FRANCISCO, BAY AREA AND CHARLESTON [2] Although they are a continent apart, the cities of Charleston, S.C., and San Francisco now share a fateful page in the ledger of time. [3] They are the scenes of two of the century's costliest, back-to-back natural disasters: Hurricane Hugo and the Bay Area earthquake. A major earthquake rocked northern California on Oct. 16, 1989 evening, collapsing part of the San Francisco Bay Bridge and shaking Candlestick Park and buildings up to 95 miles away .
