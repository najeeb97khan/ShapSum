[1] A passenger ferry sank off the Caribbean coast while being towed to port, and 59 people drowned and six are missing. [2] The ferry Justo Rufino Barrios II sank Sunday, 01/01/1989, afternoon in Amatique Bay. [3] The Justo Rufineo Barrios II ran out of fuel midway on a regular 16-mile run from the town Livingston, across the bay, to Puerto Barrios. [4] The boat sank while it was being towed by a tugboat. [5] A 40-year-old Spanish citizen, Vicente Daudi, was among the dead. [6] 13 bodies have been identified.
