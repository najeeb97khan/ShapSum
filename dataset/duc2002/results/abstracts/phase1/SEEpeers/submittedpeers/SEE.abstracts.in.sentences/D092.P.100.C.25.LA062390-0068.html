[1] AMERICAN RED CROSS'S CHRONOLOGY [2] Setting aside years of animosity, the U.S. government joined with private humanitarian groups on June 21, 1990 to funnel medicine, tents, clothing, food and other relief materials to help earthquake-stricken Iran. Confronted with reports that Iran would not welcome assistance from Israel, the groups were uncertain whether they should offer any assistance . Andy Griffel, executive director of American Jewish World Service, said directors of the assistance organization participated in a conference call on June 21, 1990 morning to debate whether to collect money for the Iranian victims .
