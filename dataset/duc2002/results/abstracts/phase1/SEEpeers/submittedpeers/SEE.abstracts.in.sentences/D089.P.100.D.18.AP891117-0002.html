[1] Federal aid was approved Friday for tornado-ravaged Huntsville, where 17 died and several hundred were injured or lost their homes, and Gov. Guy Hunt toured hospitals and visited with grieving families. [2] ``I've been around a lot of tornadoes, but I've not seen the type of damage on as broad a scale as I've seen this morning,'' Hunt said after a helicopter tour of the 10-mile-long path of destruction. [3] State emergency management officials estimated the damage in excess of $100 million in the agency's letter to President Bush requesting a disaster declaration, said Terry Abbott, the governor's press secretary.
