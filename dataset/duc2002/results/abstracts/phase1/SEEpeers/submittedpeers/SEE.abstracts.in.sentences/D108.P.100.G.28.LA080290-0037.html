[1] Here is a brief chronology in the dispute between Iraq and Kuwait over oil production and their common border July 17: Iraqi President Saddam Hussein accuses Kuwait and the United Arab Emirates of flooding the oil market and driving prices down July 18: Iraq accuses Kuwait of stealing $2.4 billion worth of oil from wells along disputed border with Iraq and seeking to wreck Iraq's economy by overproducing oil and pushing down prices July 20: Kuwaiti officials suggest Iraq's sudden outburst of belligerence is aimed at getting its creditors -- including Kuwait -- to write off billions of dollars in debts from the 1980-88 Iran-Iraq war
