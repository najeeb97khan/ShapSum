[1] A representative of the African National Congress said Saturday, 11/12/1988, the South African government may release black nationalist leader Nelson Mandela as early as Tuesday, 11/15/1988. [2] Mandela the 70-year-old leader of the ANC jailed 27 years ago, was sentenced to life in prison for conspiring to overthrow the South African government. [3] Since then, it has been widely rumored Mandela will be released by Christmas in a move to win strong international support for the South African government.
