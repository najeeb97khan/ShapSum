[1] Dave Johnson of the United States clinched the decathlon gold medal in the final event at the World University Games Sunday. [2] Johnson trailed Mikhail Medved of the Soviet Union by 11 points before the 1,500-meter race, the final event of the two-day competition. [3] Olympic steeplechase champion Julius Kariuki of Kenya, running the distance for the first time this year, won the men's 10,000-meter race, while the Romanian team earned six gold medals. [4] Kariuki, who won the gold medal at the 1988 Seoul Games in the 3,000-meter steeplechase, beat out Zeki Oeztuerk of Turkey to win the 10,000 in the slow time of 28:35.46. [5] Galina Astafei of Romania, the world junior champion, upset Silvia Costa of Cuba in the women's high jump.
