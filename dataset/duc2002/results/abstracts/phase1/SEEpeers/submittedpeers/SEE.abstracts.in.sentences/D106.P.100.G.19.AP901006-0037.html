[1] Unidentified assailants hurled a grenade and fired shots today, 10/06/1990, at the home of relatives of Nelson Mandela, killing a baby girl and injuring her parents. [2] African National Congress officials said the dead child and her parents were distant cousins of Nelson Mandela. [3] They denied South African police and press reports that the child was Mandela's great-granddaughter. [4] Police said they did not know who was responsible for the attack. [5] No group took immediate responsibility. [6] Police Lt. Govindesamy Mariemuthoo said the attack occurred before dawn at the home of Monde Mandela in the black township of Soweto.
