[1] THE annual Booker ballyhoo got going again yesterday when the judges for this year's Pounds 20,000 prize for British (and Commonwealth) fiction announced the shortlist. [2] Sacred Hunger by Barry Unsworth (Hamish Hamilton Pounds 15.99). [3] The judges will have to rule on the characterisation of the European protagonists who go off to found a Utopia in Florida.
