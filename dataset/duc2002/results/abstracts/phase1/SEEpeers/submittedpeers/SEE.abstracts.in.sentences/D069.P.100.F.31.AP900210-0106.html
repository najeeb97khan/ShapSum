[1] About 20,000 East Germans, many carrying West German flags, demonstrated Saturday for speedy German reunification, the official East German news agency ADN said. [2] Earlier Saturday, West German Finance Minister Theo Waigel said the East German economy was in a ``sorrowful state'' and urged the East German government to release details on its financial situation. [3] ADN said the demonstrators held signs saying ``Gorbi, what about disarmament''? and ``Tanks out, helicopters in''? [4] The protesters, from the East German town of Prenzlau, claimed the Soviets had backed out of disarmament plans by replacing a tank unit that had been stationed at a military base there with helicopters, ADN reported.
