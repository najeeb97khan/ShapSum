[1] Hirohito was born on April 29, 1901. [2] Hirohito studied government and law with private tutors. [3] Hirohito became prince regent on Nov. 25, 1921, after his father became a mental and physical invalid, became emperor five years later and was formally enthroned in 1928. Hirohito died Saturday at the age of 87. [4] He was succeeded by Akihito, his eldest son. [5] World leaders mourned death of Emperor Hirohito. [6] To the rest of the world, he was known as Emperor Hirohito, but in Japan his given name was seldom used. [7] In Japanese he was simply called Tenno Heika, meaning His Majesty The Emperor .
