[1] Six novels have been nominated for the Booker Prize, Britain's most prestigious fiction award, and bookmakers say the favorite is ``The Remains of the Day'' by Japanese author Kazuo Ishiguro. [2] The other books nominated are: _ ``Cat's Eye,'' a story of painter Elaine Risley and her return to her childhood home in Toronto by Canadian author Margaret Atwood, also a previous nominee. [3] _ ``The Book of Evidence,'' a prison story of kidnap and murder convict Freddie Montgomery by John Banville, literary editor of The Irish Times newspaper. [4] _ ``Jigsaw,'' by Sybille Bedford, an autobiographical novel about an adolescent's travels through Germany, Italy and France.
