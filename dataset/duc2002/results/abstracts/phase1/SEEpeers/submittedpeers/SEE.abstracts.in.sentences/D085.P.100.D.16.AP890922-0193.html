[1] Sept. 12 _ Storm reaches the 39 mph tropical storm threshold and is named Hugo. [2] Sept. 13 _ Hugo develops into a hurricane as winds reach 74 mph. [3] Sept. 16 _ Hugo bears down on Guadeloupe and Dominica with winds reaching 140 mph. [4] Sept. 21 (midnight) _ Hugo crashes into Charleston, S.C., with winds of 135 mph, leveling buildings and flooding streets. [5] Twelve hours later it had all but lost the characteristics of a tropical storm and was weakening, although the weather system featured maximum sustained winds of 40 mph and torrential rains.
