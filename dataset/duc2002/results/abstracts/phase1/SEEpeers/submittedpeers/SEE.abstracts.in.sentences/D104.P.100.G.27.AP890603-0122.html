[1] Here is a chronology of events that began Saturday in Tiananmen Square: 1 a.m. [2] Military units also seen approaching from the west and the north. [3] 2 a.m. [4] _ The main body of troops is stopped several hundred yards east of the vast square by thousands of people. [5] 2 a.m. to 9 a.m. [6] _ Troops straggle away from their positions near the square, walking in small groups followed by watchful residents. [7] 12 noon _ Two truckloads of People's Armed Police attack crowds outside Beijing Hotel east of the square and up to 30 people injured. [8] 2 p.m. [9] _ About 5,000 troops emerge from Great Hall of the People on the western side of the square and are surrounded by crowds.
