[1] In a dramatic finish, the San Francisco 49ers football team won the Super Bowl on Sunday with a 20-16 victory over the Cincinnati Bengals. [2] Joe Montana's 10-yard touchdown pass to John Taylor with 34 seconds left provided the winning margin. [3] The winning score came at the end of a 92-yard drive, engineered by Montana, whose touchdown pass gave him a Super Bowl record with 357 yards passing. [4] It also gave 49ers Coach Bill Walsh his third Super Bowl win.
