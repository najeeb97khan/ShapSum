[1] FT 04 FEB 94 / Maxwell musical injunction could stop the show Sir Nicholas Lyell QC, the attorney-general, will attempt to stop the curtain rising on Maxwell: The Musical, the West End show chronicling the life and times of the late Robert Maxwell. [2] Press coverage of the late publisher since his death has already caused deep concern to the Maxwell brothers' lawyers. Mr Alun Jones QC, Kevin Maxwell's barrister, led calls for reporting restrictions to be placed on a recent High Court ruling that Robert Maxwell had been fraudulent in his dealings with shares in Berlitz, the publishing company .
