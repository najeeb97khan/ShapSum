[1] Iraq's powerful army invaded this small oil-rich kingdom early today, 08/02/1990. [2] Tank-led troops quickly seized the ruler's palace and government buildings, and the emir fled to Saudi Arabia. [3] The Iraqi government of President Saddam Hussein claimed it invaded at the request of revolutionaries who had already staged a coup and established "The Provisional Government of Free Kuwait". [4] The assault followed two weeks of tension between Kuwait and Iraq, caused by Iraqi accusations that Kuwait stole oil from a disputed border area. [5] President Bush signed orders freezing Iraqi assets in the United States and blocking most Iraqi imports, including oil.
