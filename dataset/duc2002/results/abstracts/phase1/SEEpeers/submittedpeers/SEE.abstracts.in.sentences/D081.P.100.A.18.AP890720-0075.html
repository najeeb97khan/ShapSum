[1] Strikes by tens of thousands of miners in this Siberian coal region spread to four more of the Soviet Union's coal fields, Tass reported today. [2] The official news agency said coal miners in Vorkuta in the far north, the Don River city of Rostov in southwestern Russia and the Ukrainian industrial center of Dnepropetrovsk walked out Wednesday. [3] In Vorkuta, strikers sent a letter to the Kremlin asking for an ``urgent resolution of the sharp economic and social problems of the coal sector, and also specific problems of workers of the far north,'' Tass said.
