[1] THE STORY OF SAKHAROV [2] In a major rebuff to the Soviet Union's scientific establishment, Soviet physicists on Jan. 19, 1989 nominated Nobel laureate Andrei D. Sakharov to run as a candidate in nationwide parliamentary elections scheduled for March. Although Sakharov has publicly expressed his support for President Mikhail S. Gorbachev, in recent months Sakharov seems to have become increasingly concerned about the slow and uneven pace of reform. [3] In a question-and-answer session with physicists, he described the political situation in the Soviet Union as "extremely complicated" .
