[1] THE STORY OF LOVE LUCY [2] Desi himself, Vivian Vance and William Frawley -- were superb farceurs themselves, but Lucy was the dynamo that made it all work. Lucy was all-American but, equally, a citizen of the world . She had had bit parts in two earlier films but "Roman Scandals" was the one that gave her a firmer foothold on the slippery slopes of a Hollywood career . She perfected her matchless craft as a comedienne in dozens of films, including three of Fred Astaire's and one of the Marx Brothers ("Room Service") .
