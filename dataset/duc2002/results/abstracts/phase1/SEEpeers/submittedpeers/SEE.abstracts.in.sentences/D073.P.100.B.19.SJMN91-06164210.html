[1] Mount Pinatubo erupted with six thundering explosions today, 06/12/1991, shooting a giant plume of ash 15 miles high. [2] Today(06/12/1991)'s first eruption began at 8:40 a.m. [3] It was followed by a second explosion a few minutes later and a third, smaller blast at 11:49 a.m. [4] The huge plume could be seen in Manila. [5] Two more thundering explosions rocked Mount Pinatubo late today, 06/12/1991. [6] The explosions occurred at 10:52 p.m. and 11:05 p.m. [7] The second explosion sent a plume of ash and steam 80,000 feet into the sky. [8] Shortly after midnight, a sixth explosion was reported.
