[1] Cynthia Lennon joins the throng denouncing the new, unauthorized biography of her late former husband, John Lennon, as written by a money-hungry author capitalizing on untruths. [2] The biography by Albert Goldman, "The Lives of John Lennon," depicts the former Beatle as an anorexic bisexual addled by drugs who raged his way from Liverpool to New York. [3] Cynthia and John's son, Julian Lennon, also denounced the book on the radio taping, calling it "lies, untruths," and adding that "the whole thing is just sickening". [4] Sean Lennon, the son of Yoko and John who is now 12, added his voice on the CBS broadcast, rebutting charges that his father was inattentive to parenting.
