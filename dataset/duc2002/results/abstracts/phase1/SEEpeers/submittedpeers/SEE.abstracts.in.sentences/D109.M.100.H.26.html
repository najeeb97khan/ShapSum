[1] (06/19/1994) This capital of central China's Hunan province has been hit by floods in the wake of a succession of rainstorms on the upper and middle reaches of the Xiangjiang River. [2] (06/19/1994) The flood has caused serious losses to people along both banks of the river, especially in Changsha, according to the sources. [3] (06/19/1994) Most of the 15,000 people affected by the flood there have been evacuated to safer places. [4] (06/22/1994) Flood lasting for ten or more days in Fujian Province has brought about heavy losses in agriculture there. [5] (06/22/1994) Of these, Sanming City is worst hit by the flood.
