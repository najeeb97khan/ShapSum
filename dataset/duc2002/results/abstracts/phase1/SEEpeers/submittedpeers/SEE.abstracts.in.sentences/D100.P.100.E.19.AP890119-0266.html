[1] Published in North America last fall, ` ` John Lennon, My Brother'' was co - authored by Giuliano, a Beatles aficionado. [2] Giuliano wanted to do a book about Lennon. [3] Baird wanted to do a book about the ex - Beatle 's maligned mother, whose name also was Julia.
