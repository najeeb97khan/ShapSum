[1] Time ran out for the U.S. athletes when the Winter Olympics ended Sunday, with a team headed by Brent Rushlaw a tick away from a U.S. bobsled medal and Dutch speed skater Yvonne van Gennip a triple gold medalist with time to spare. [2] The 250 figure skaters who performed included past and present medal winners as well as young skaters from Albertville, France, site of the 1992 Winter Games, and Seoul, South Korea, site of this year's summer Games. [3] Van Gennip took more than six seconds off her own world record in the 5,000 meters to win her third gold medal. [4] Finland won the hockey silver medal, handing the Soviets their first loss of the Games, 2-1.
