[1] The Communist Party today admitted that East Germany's socialist system has failed, and expressed support for a type of partnership with West Germany. [2] The statement in the Communist Party newspaper Neues Deutschland was one of the bluntest official assessments to date of the country's increasingly troubled situation. [3] It came a day after shouting matches broke out over German reunification during pro-democracy protests in Leipzig and Dresden. [4] Neues Deutschland, in proposing a new party platform, said ``socialism has definitely failed as a social system in our country'' and acknowledged that the ``popular revolutionary movement'' of the past weeks had led to the scrapping of the old system.
