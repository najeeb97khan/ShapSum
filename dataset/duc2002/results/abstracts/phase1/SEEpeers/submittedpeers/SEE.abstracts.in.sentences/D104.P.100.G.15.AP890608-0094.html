[1] April 18 _ Thousands of students march in Beijing and Shanghai, expressing dissatisfaction with the government and shouting "Long live Hu Yaobang! April 27 _ In the largest to date of a continuing series of protests, more than 150,000 students, with wide support from people on the street, surge past police lines and fill Tiananmen Square, chanting slogans for democracy and freedom. [2] May 13 _ About 2,000 students begin a hunger strike in Tiananmen Square. [3] May 14 _ Thousands flock to the square to back the students. [4] May 15 _ The government deadline for students to leave the square passes. [5] Students reject his appeal and hold marches that generate 1 million supporters in Beijing.
