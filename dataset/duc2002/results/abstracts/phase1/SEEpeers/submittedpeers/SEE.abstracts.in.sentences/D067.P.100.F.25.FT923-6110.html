[1] HURRICANE AFFECTS NEW ORLEANS, LOUISIANA AND FLORIDA [2] Damage caused by Hurricane Andrew could rise to $20bn, it was estimated on Aug. 25, 1992, as one of the costliest US storms this century threatened a further devastating landfall near the city of New Orleans. [3] The hurricane tore through southern Florida early on Aug. 23, 1992 morning, causing billions of dollars of property damage and at least 12 deaths, and on Aug. 25, 1992 was moving north-west across the Gulf of Mexico with winds of about 140 miles a hour. [4] More than 24 hours after the hurricane, some 825,000 households and businesses were still without power.
