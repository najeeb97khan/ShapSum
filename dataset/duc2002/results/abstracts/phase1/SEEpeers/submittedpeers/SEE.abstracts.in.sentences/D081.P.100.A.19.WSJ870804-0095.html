[1] The National Union of Mineworkers said an estimated 200,000 black gold and coal miners in South Africa will begin a strike Sunday 08/09/1987 night for higher wages and better working conditions. [2] The union is demanding a 30%, wage increase. [3] In addition, the union is demanding pay for black miners doing dangerous work. [4] The black miners also are asking for 30 days' leave each year, and a paid holiday on June 16, the anniversary of the 1976 Soweto riots. [5] They also want an increase in death benefits to five years from two.
