[1] >From behind bars, Nelson Mandela has dominated the fight for black rights in South Africa. [2] 1944 Mandela joins the African National Congress at age 26, later becoming president of the group's Youth League. [3] January, 1962 Attends Pan-African Freedom Movement Conference in Ethiopia; travels to London to meet with leftists. [4] July, 1963 With Mandela in jail, police raid ANC offices, seizing documents outlining guerrilla campaign. [5] January, 1985 President Pieter W. Botha offers to free Mandela if he renounces violence; Mandela refuses until the government takes steps to dismantle apartheid and grants full political rights to blacks.
