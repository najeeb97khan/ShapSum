[1] BLAST KILLS 47 ON BATTLESHIP IOWA; A fiery explosion in a giant gun turret rocked the refitted battleship Iowa on maneuvers in the Atlantic on 04/19/1989, killing at least 47 sailors and injuring an undetermined number, the Pentagon said. None of the guns in the no. 2 turret had been fired before the explosion instantly killed the seamen, Navy officials said. [2] Maj. Tom Johnston, a spokesman for the Atlantic Fleet in Norfolk, said he had no information on the causes of death. [3] The bodies of 47 sailors killed in the gun explosion on the Iowa arrived in the United States on Thursday, 04/20/1989 .
