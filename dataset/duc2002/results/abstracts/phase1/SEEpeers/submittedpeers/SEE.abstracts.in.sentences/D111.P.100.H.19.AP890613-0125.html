[1] A yacht built for Adolf Hitler and sunk with great fanfare to commemorate the "Voyage of the Damned" was mistakenly dropped on a delicate coral reef in a shallow shipping lane. [2] The 85-foot Ostwind yacht was supposed to become an artificial reef in 250 feet of water about two miles off the coast. [3] It was sunk 06/04/1989. [4] The sinking site was supposed to be near where the Coast Guard intercepted the St. Louis. [5] The Ostwind was pushed off a barge about a mile north of the site, and is resting in 25 feet of water on a living reef.
