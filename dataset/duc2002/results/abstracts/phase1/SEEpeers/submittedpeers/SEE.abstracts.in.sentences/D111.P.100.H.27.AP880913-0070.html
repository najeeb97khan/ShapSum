[1] A freighter carrying a cargo of beer sank in the North Sea today off the Dutch coast and all nine crew members were rescued from life rafts, a coast guard spokesman said. [2] The coast guard official, who spoke in a Dutch radio interview, identified the ship involved as the Finnish-registered freighter Ra and said it had been carrying a cargo of beer. [3] The nine crew members boarded two life rafts after the sinking in high winds. [4] They were taken ashore by a West German helicopter from the island of Borkum, about 30 miles from the site of the sinking, the official said.
