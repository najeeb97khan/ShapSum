[1] Pakistani troops fired on Indian security forces who stopped a mob of Pakistani protesters from storming across the border into disputed Kashmir, officials said today. [2] At least three demonstrators were killed Sunday night, a Defense Ministry spokesman said in Islamabad. [3] Pakistan's state-run TV said the Indian guards killed as many as six. [4] The trouble started after Indian border guards opened fire on hundreds of Pakistanis who tried to cross a cease-fire line separating Pakistan-controlled Kashmir from the Indian-held region. [5] A spokesman for the Indian Defense Ministry said Pakistani troops then fired about 200 rounds from light and medium weapons at Indian border posts in the Uri sector.
