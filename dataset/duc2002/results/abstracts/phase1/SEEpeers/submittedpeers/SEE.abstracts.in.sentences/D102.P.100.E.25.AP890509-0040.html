[1] LUCY'S CHRONOLOGY [2] Two of the three long-running comedy shows in which Miss Ball starred, "The Lucy Show" and "Here's Lucy," aired on May 7, 1989 nights. [3] In Chicago, the Rev. Jack Wall told people at Old St. Patrick Church that God gave the world talents like Miss Ball "to show us life is not a tragedy, but a comedy with a happy ending". [4] Florists delivered arrangements including a 3-foot heart of red carnations with the words "I Love Lucy" written across. [5] All three memorial Masses began at 8 p.m. on May 7, 1989, the time Miss Ball's three most popular programs were often broadcast.
