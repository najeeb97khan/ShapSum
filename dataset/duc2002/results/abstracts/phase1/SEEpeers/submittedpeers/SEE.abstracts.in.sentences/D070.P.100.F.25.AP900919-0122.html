[1] THE STORY OF HONECKER [2] East Germany's ousted secret police chief threatened to break former Communist leader Erich Honecker's neck when the two faced each other in jail, a West German newspaper reported on Sept. 18, 1990. [3] One prison warden told Bild that many of the guards had worked for Mielke's Stasi and that they might interpret his threat against Honecker as an order to kill the former Communist chief. [4] Mielke screamed at Honecker "I'll break your neck"! the newspaper quoted witnesses as describing the scene when the two met in a East German jail. [5] Honecker was released from jail for health reasons.
