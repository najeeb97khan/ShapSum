[2] Yeltsin has drastically tipped the balance of power, showing himself to be at least the political equal, if not the master, of Gorbachev. [3] "And now for a bit of relaxation," Yeltsin said, interrupting Gorbachev's speech. [4] "Let me sign a decree suspending the activity of the Russian Communist Party".
