[1] 1953 _ "I Love Lucy" episode in which Lucy Ricardo gives birth to Little Ricky is telecast the same day that Lucille Ball, in real life, gives birth to second child, Desi Jr. 1962 _ Miss Ball goes solo, returning to the airwaves in "The Lucy Show". [2] 1968 _ "The Lucy Show" is redesigned and becomes "Here's Lucy". [3] 1974 _ Miss Ball plays the title role in the movie version of "Mame". [4] 1985 _ In her first serious dramatic role in years, Miss Ball plays a New York bag lady in the CBS-TV movie "Stone Pillow".
