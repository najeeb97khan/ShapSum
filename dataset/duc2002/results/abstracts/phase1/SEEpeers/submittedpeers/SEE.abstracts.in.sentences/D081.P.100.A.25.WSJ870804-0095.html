[1] MINES AND STRIKES [2] The National Union of Mineworkers said an estimated 200,000 black gold and coal miners in South Africa will begin a strike on Aug. 1, 1987 night for higher wages and better working conditions, in what it said would be the largest job action ever to affect the mining industry in that nation=2E The strike initially would cover more than half of South Africa's gold mines and about one-fifth of its coal mines, said Cyril Ramaphosa, the union's general secretary.
