[1] TO MOST PEOPLE, marathons and vacations are mutually exclusive. [2] Vacation, according to Webster, means " freedom from any activity; rest; respite ". [3] A marathon, says the same source, is " a footrace of 26 miles, 385 yards, run over an open course ". [4] But a unique subset has formed: people who like to do both at the same time. [5] The cities are different, but most marathons include a carbo - loading pasta party the night before, medical exams, a post - race celebration, commemorative T - shirts and awards to finishers.
