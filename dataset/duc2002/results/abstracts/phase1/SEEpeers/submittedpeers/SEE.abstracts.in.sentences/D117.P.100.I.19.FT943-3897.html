[1] This week (09/12/1994-09/18/1994) the shortlist for the hypothetical 1894 Booker Prize will be announced. [2] They have avoided dull and worthy classics and chosen six books which stirred the fin de siecle world and remain compelling today: George du Maurier's Trilby, Anthony Hope's The Prisoner of Zenda, George Moore's Esther Waters, Kipling's Jungle Book, George Gissing's In the Year of Jubilee and RL Stevenson's The Ebb Tide. [3] The winner will be revealed on 10/15/1994 (four days after the 1994 Booker Prize ceremony) at the Cheltenham Festival of Literature.
