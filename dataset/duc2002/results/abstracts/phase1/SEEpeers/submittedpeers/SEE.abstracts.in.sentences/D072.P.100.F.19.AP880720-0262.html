[1] Actresses Lauren Bacall, Betty Comden and Phyllis Newman are among performers in the birthday bash being thrown in 08/1988 for conductor Leonard Bernstein at the music center where he got his start. [2] The Leonard Bernstein Gala Birthday Performance is a four-day affair beginning on the composer's 70th birthday, 08/25/1988, to raise money for the music center. [3] The concert will also celebrate Bernstein 's accomplishments in popular music with excerpts from such works as "West Side Story" and "On the Town".
