[1] Although they are a continent apart, the cities of Charleston, S.C., and San Francisco now share a fateful page in the ledger of time. [2] They are the scenes of two of the century 's costliest, back-to-back natural disasters: Hurricane Hugo and the Bay Area earthquake. [3] There are dramatic similarities and profound differences in the ways these two cities were affected and altered in the first week after they were ravaged by two powerful forces of nature -- one that angrily swept across the land from the sea and one that violently rumbled beneath the earth.
