[1] Here is a look at key events in the student pro-democracy movement and at events since Sunday's assault by soldiers on protesters in Tiananmen Square: On 04/15/1989 former Communist Party chief Hu Yaobang dies. [2] Beijing University students put up posters praising him and indirectly criticizing opponents who forced his resignation after student demonstrations in 1986 - 87. [3] On 05/13/1989 about 2,000 students begin a hunger strike in Tiananmen Square. [4] On 05/15/1989 the government deadline for students to leave the square passes. [5] On 05/20/1989 martial law is declared in parts of Beijing.
