[1] Drummer Andy Newmark was touring with Roxy Music in the summer of 1980 when he got the call that changed his life: He was to play on John Lennon's new album. [2] Lennon had been in virtual seclusion for five years, busy taking care of his son, Sean, and admittedly out of songwriting ideas. [3] But in 1980, he suddenly felt inspired again and planned to return to the studio to make an album with his wife, Yoko Ono.
