[1] BFN [Text] Changsha, June 19 (XINHUA) -- This capital of central China's Hunan province has been hit by floods in the wake of a succession of rainstorms on the upper and middle reaches of the Xiangjiang River. [2] The water level of the river, which flows through Changsha, is still rising sharply, according to local sources. [3] The flood has caused serious losses to people along both banks of the river, especially in Changsha, according to the sources. [4] In the early hours of yesterday six villages and one fishing ground at Yulushan township were flooded when an embankment collapsed.
