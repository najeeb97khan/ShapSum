[1] Moslems Mourn Slain Militant Leader, Vow to Continue Secession Fight SRINAGAR, India (AP) A leader of the Moslem separatist movement in Kashmir tried to hurl a grenade into a police bunker Friday but was killed in a volley of police gunfire. [2] Tens of thousands of Moslems gathered later for the funeral. [3] Comrades fired into the air and vowed to carry on the fight for independence from India. [4] Ashfaq Majid Wani, 28, was a leader of the Jammu-Kashmir Liberation Front, a leading separatist group. [5] Soldiers and police prevented the procession and took Wani's body to a burial ground in a government ambulance escorted by a police jeep.
