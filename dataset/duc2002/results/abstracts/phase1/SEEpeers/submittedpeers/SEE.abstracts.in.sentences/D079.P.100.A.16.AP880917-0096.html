[1] Rains from Hurricane Gilbert sent a river raging over its banks in Monterrey, sweeping at least 10 policemen to their deaths and overturning buses loaded with evacuees from Matamoros, police said today. [2] At 9 a.m. EDT today, Gilbert's center was located near latitude 25.1 north and longitude 100.3 west, or 185 miles west southwest of Brownsville and 40 miles south of Monterrey, said the National Hurricane Center in Florida. [3] The Sierra de Tamaulipas, a small mountain range between Monterrey and the coast, should blunt the hurricane's strength, said Mark Zimmer, a meterologist at the National Hurricane Center.
