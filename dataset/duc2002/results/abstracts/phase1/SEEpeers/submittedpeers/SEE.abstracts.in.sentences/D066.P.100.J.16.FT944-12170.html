[1] FT 02 NOV 94 / FT Review of Business Books (10): Few better qualified - What is most startling about Wal-Mart is the speed of its growth / Sam Walton's American dream 'WAL-MART: A HISTORY OF SAM WALTON'S RETAIL PHENOMENON' by Sandra S. Vance and Roy V. Scott Twayne Publishers, New York, Dollars 14.95 240 pages Many people might be said to be the personification of the American Dream; few are better qualified for that title than Sam Walton. [2] But perhaps the most fundamental reason for its success, the book suggests, has been Wal-Mart's streamlined and highly responsive management structure.
