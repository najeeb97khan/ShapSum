[1] The Berlin villa where the Nazi leadership met to implement the ``final solution'' against Europe's Jewish population will be the site of a World Jewish Congress conference on German reunification. [2] The New York-based organization announced Tuesday, 03/20/1990, that the meeting is scheduled for May 8, the 45th anniversary of V-E Day. [3] Some 100 members of the World Jewish Congress from about 70 countries will attend the conference at the villa in Wannsee, Berlin, where on Jan. 20, 1942, high German government and party officials adopted a plan to exterminate the Jews.
