[1] US CITIES along the Gulf of Mexico from Alabama to eastern Texas were on storm watch 08/24/1992 night as Hurricane Andrew headed west after sweeping across southern Florida, causing at least eight deaths and severe property damage. [2] The hurricane was one of the fiercest in the US in decades and the first to hit Miami directly in a quarter of a century. [3] In the Bahamas, government spokesman Mr Jimmy Curry said four deaths had been reported on outlying eastern islands.
