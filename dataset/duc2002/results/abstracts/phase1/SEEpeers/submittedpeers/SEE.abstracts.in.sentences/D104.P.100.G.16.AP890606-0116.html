[1] April 18 _ Thousands of students march in Beijing and Shanghai, expressing dissatisfaction with the government and shouting ``Long live Hu Yaobang! April 27 _ In the largest to date of a continuing series of protests, more than 150,000 students, with wide support from people on the street, surge past police lines and fill Tiananmen Square, chanting slogans for democracy and freedom. [2] June 3-4 _ Tens of thousands of troops make several attempts to move on the square but are driven back by crowds of hundreds of thousands of people supporting the students.
