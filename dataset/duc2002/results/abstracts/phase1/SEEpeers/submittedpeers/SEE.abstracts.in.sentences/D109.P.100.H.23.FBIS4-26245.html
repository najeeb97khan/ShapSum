[1] BFN [Text] Beijing, June 20 (XINHUA) -- Floods continue to have serious effects in Guangdong, Hunan and Jiangxi Provinces and the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region in southern China. [2] Information from the State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters revealed that the flood peak on the Xijiang River today reached Guangdong Province. [3] By eight o'clock yesterday morning, a peak flood water level of 25.91 m [meters] was recorded at the flood monitoring station of Guangxi's Wuzhou city, 10.91 m above the danger level.
