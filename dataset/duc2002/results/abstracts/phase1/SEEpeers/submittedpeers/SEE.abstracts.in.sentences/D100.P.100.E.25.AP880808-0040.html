[1] THE STORY OF LENNON [2] John Lennon spent much of a three-year span tucked away in his room, sleeping or sitting in a lotus position with his head enveloped in tobacco or marijuana smoke, according to a biography of the slain Beatle. [3] An excerpt from the book, "The Lives of John Lennon" by journalist and biographer Albert Goldman, appearing in the Aug. 15 issue of People magazine, discloses a sometimes unflattering portrait of both Lennon and his second wife, Yoko Ono. [4] Lennon rarely wore clothing, other than a pair of backless slippers, and avoided touching anyone, according to the book.
