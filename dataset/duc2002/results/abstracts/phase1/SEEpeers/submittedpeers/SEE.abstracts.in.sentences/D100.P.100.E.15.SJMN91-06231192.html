[1] In 1966, Ono and Lennon met. [2] Ono and Lennon were married in 1970. [3] There has been much speculation on Ono and Lennon's relationship. [4] Others say Lennon used his relationship with Ono as an escape from things in his life that he felt were controlling him -- such as the Beatles. [5] In May 1968, when the Beatles came together at the studio to work on "The Beatles," the two-record set commonly called "The White Album," Ono was at Lennon's side. [6] According to at least one Beatles biographer, Ono even accompanied Lennon on trips to the bathroom.
