[1] In the Pakistani capital of Islamabad, meanwhile, India and Pakistan opened negotiations today aimed at repairing relations threatened by the rebellion in the disputed northern region. [2] Relations between Pakistan and India worsened in January when Indian soldiers cracked down on Moslem militants in Kashmir who are seeking independence from India or union with Pakistan. [3] India has accused Pakistan of arming and training the militants, a charge Pakistan has denied. [4] Pakistan denies it is training or helping the militants, but calls the separatist struggle a fight for self-determination. [5] India and Pakistan have fought two previous wars over Kashmir, in 1948 and 1965.
