[1] Dave Johnson of the United States clinched the decathlon gold medal in the final event at the World University Games Sunday. Johnson trailed Mikhail Medved of the Soviet Union by 11 points before the 1,500-meter race, the final event of the two-day competition . Olympic steeplechase champion Julius Kariuki of Kenya, running the distance for the first time this year, won the men's 10,000-meter race, while the Romanian team earned six gold medals . Reggie Davis won the gold and Kevin Henderson took the bronze for the U.S. in the men's 400-meter intermediate hurdles .
