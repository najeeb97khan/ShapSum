[1] Dawn broke Sunday 05/29/1989 over a battle-scarred Tiananmen Square with tanks and rifle-toting troops ruling the vast expanse occupied on 05/28/1989 by students seeking a freer society from their communist rulers. [2] Hundreds of troops were deployed on the monument to revolutionary heroes in the square's center and thousands more at important intersections nearby. [3] The battle was primarily fought in the streets leading to the square. [4] The few thousand students remaining in the square stayed calm as they quietly gathered on the steps of the heroes' monument. [5] The state-run speaker warned that troops would use every means to clear the square.
