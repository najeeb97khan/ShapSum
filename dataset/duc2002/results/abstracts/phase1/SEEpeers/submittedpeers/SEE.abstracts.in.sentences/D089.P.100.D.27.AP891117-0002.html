[1] Federal aid was approved Friday for tornado-ravaged Huntsville, where 17 died and several hundred were injured or lost their homes, and Gov. Guy Hunt toured hospitals and visited with grieving families. [2] ``I've been around a lot of tornadoes, but I've not seen the type of damage on as broad a scale as I've seen this morning,'' Hunt said after a helicopter tour of the 10-mile-long path of destruction. [3] Hunt visited privately Friday morning with families of some of the 17 killed in the tornado, The victims ranged in age from 7 to 67.
