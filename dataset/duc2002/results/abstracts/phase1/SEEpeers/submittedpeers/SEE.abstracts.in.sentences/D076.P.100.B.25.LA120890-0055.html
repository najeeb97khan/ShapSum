[1] THE STORY OF THATCHER [2] Queen Elizabeth II awarded former Prime Minister Margaret Thatcher the coveted Order of Merit on Dec. 6, 1990 and made her husband a baronet, which is a hereditary knighthood. Thatcher said she was "deeply honored and moved" by the award . At the same time, the queen gave a baronetcy to Thatcher's husband Denis, the subject of fictional letters from No. 10 Downing Street invented by the satirical magazine Private Eye about life as the prime minister's consort .
