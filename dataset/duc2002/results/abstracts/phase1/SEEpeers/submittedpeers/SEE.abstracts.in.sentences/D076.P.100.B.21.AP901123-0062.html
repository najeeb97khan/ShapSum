[1] Former President Reagan thanked British Prime Minister Margaret Thatcher for backing him up when other allies backed down, heaping praise on his political soulmate as she heads off into the sunset. [2] Mrs. Thatcher on Thursday succumbed to the challenge mounted by Michael Heseltine, who resigned as her defense minister in 1986. [3] They often stood side-by-side at international gatherings and called each other Ronnie and Margaret. [4] Two years ago, as they met for the final time before Reagan left office, the president said he was leaving the White House with ``considerable satisfaction in the knowledge that Margaret Thatcher will still reside at No. 10 Downing Street''.
