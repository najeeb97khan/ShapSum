Discovery's carefully scripted countdown began on time at 3 p.m. [2] The ship's five-member crew is set to blast off at 5:31 a.m. PDT Tuesday, 14 days after the flight was grounded just four minutes before takeoff by a faulty hydraulic power steering unit .
