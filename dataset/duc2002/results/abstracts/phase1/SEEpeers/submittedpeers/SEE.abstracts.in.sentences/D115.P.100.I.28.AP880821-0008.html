[1] 100 Feared Dead In Earthquake That Hits Nepal And Eastern India A strong earthquake hit the Himalayan kingdom of Nepal and parts of eastern India on Sunday morning, triggering landslides and collapsing buildings. [2] The department said the quake was centered near Udayapur, a town near the Indian-Nepalese border and 100 miles southeast of Katmandu, Nepal's capital. [3] The state-run Radio Nepal said at least 27 people were killed in the towns of Bhaktapur and Dharan when they were buried by collapsing houses. [4] Press Trust of India news agency said that in addition to 100 feared dead in India and Nepal, 1,000 people were injured in the Indian states of Bihar, Sikkim and West Bengal.
