[1] Chinese troops stormed Tiananmen Square 05/28/1989, shooting and beating their way through crowds of pro-democracy protesters in clashes that witnesses and doctors said killed at least 32 people. [2] Hundreds were wounded. [3] The exact number of people killed may never be known. [4] They range from 500 to 12,000 people. [5] Nearly all of the deaths and injuries occurred Saturday night and early Sunday when troops, firing randomly into large crowds, smashed through barricades to reach Tiananmen square and attack demonstrators demanding democratic reforms.
