[1] TPA AND ANGIOPLASTIES [2] Doctors from the University of Michigan Medical Center studied the effects of sending a few carefully selected heart patients home early. [3] They found that these people had no unusual complications, got back to work sooner and had substantially lower medical bills. [4] Topol said the early discharges were made possible by two recent medical advances tissue plasminogen activator, or TPA, and angioplasty. [5] The report represents the latest in a steady three-decade trend of sending heart attack patients home from the hospital after ever shorter stays.
