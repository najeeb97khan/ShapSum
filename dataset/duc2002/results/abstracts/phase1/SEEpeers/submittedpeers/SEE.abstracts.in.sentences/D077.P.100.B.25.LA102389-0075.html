[1] EARTHQUAKE AFFECTS CHARLESTON, SAN FRANCISCO AND SOUTH CAROLINA [2] Although they are a continent apart, the cities of Charleston, S.C., and San Francisco now share a fateful page in the ledger of time. [3] They are the scenes of two of the century's costliest, back-to-back natural disasters: Hurricane Hugo and the Bay Area earthquake. Broken glass and other debris left the roadsides lined with cars stopped by flat tires . By contrast the streets in most of San Francisco were passable within a day of the earthquake .
