[1] The shortlist of six for the Pounds 20,000 Booker Prize for fiction, announced yesterday 10/11/1994, immediately prompted the question 'Who?'. [2] The six include Alan Hollinghurst's The Folding Star, The Reef by Romesh Gunesekera and How Late It Was, How Late by James Kelman. [3] As for the other three - Knowledge of Angels by Jill Paton Walsh, Paradise by Zanzibar - born writer Abdulrazak Gurnah and Beside the Ocean of Time by George Mackay Brown - 'frankly, they don't make the grade'. [4] The winner, selected from an original 130, will be announced on 10/11/1994.
