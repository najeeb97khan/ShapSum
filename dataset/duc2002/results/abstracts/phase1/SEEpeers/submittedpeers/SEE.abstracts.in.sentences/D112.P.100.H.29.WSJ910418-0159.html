[1] LONDON -- Following his takeover of the unprofitable New York Daily News, British publisher Robert Maxwell plans to raise around #250 million ($448 million) through the sale of shares in his Mirror Group Newspapers Ltd. of Britain. [2] In a flotation organized by Samuel Montagu & Co., Mirror is to sell 45% to 49% of its shares to investors in Britain and abroad. [3] Terms will be set April 30. [4] The offering is expected to value Mirror Group at more than #500 million. Proceeds from the share flotation will be used to strengthen and refinance the group's balance sheet .
