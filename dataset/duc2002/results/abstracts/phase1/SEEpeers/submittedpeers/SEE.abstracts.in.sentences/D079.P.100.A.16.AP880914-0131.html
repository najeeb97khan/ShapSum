[1] Hurricane Gilbert battered the resorts of the Yucatan Peninsula today with 160 mph winds and torrential rains. [2] Gilbert, which has killed at least 11 people, moved over the island of Cozumel at 10 a.m. EDT, said Jose Pereira, a spokesman for the governor's office in Merida, capital of Yucatan state. [3] Gilbert is a Category 5 storm, the strongest and deadliest type of hurricane. [4] Only two Category 5 hurricanes have hit the United States _ the 1935 storm that killed 408 people in Florida and Hurricane Camille that devastated the Mississippi coast in 1969 and killed 256 people.
