[1] Hirohito was born on April 29, 1901. [2] Hirohito studied government and law with private tutors. [3] Hirohito became prince regent on Nov. 25, 1921, became emperor five years later and was formally enthroned in 1928. Hirohito died Saturday at the age of 87. [4] He was succeeded by Akihito, his eldest son .
