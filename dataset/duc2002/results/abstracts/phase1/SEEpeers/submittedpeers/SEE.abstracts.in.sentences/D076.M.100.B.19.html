[1] ``Margaret Thatcher will be seen with Winston Churchill as the greatest British prime minister of the last 50 years. [2] She was elected in 1979, the first female prime minister in Europe, and won re-election in 1983 and in 1987, when she said she planned to ``go on and on''. [3] She led her party to victory in three elections, steered it through the war with Argentina to reclaim the Falklands, faced down the miners union in a long strike, moved -- often drastically -- to reverse Britain's economic decline and won a prominent place on the world stage.
