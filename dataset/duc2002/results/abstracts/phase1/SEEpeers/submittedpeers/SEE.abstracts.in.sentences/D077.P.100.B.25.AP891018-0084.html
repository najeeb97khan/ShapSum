[1] EARTHQUAKE AFFECTS BAY AREA, SAN FRANCISCO AND SANTA CRUZ MOUNTAINS [2] Ever since the 1906 earthquake devastated San Francisco, many scientists have believed the next "big one" would strike the Los Angeles end of the mighty San Andreas Fault. [3] But studies in recent years have reminded the Bay Area that it faced another seismic disaster, and those studies became a reality on Oct. 16, 1989 when a quake measuring 6.9 on the Richter scale rocked the Bay area.
