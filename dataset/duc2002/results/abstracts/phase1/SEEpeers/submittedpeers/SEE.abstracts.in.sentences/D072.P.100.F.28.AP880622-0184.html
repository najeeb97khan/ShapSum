[1] Beverly Sills, Lauren Bacall, Betty Comden and Phyllis Newman are among performers who will sing, act and make guest appearances at a birthday bash in August for conductor Leonard Bernstein. [2] The Leonard Bernstein Gala Birthday Performance is a benefit concert scheduled for the composer's 70th birthday, Aug. 25, to raise money for the Tanglewood Music Center, where Bernstein got his conducting start. [3] The concert will celebrate Bernstein's accomplishments in popular music with excerpts from ``West Side Story,'' ``On the Town'' and others. [4] Dame Gwyneth Jones and Frederica von Stade will be among those performing highlights from ``Fidelio,'' ``Tristan und Isolde'' and other works to honor Bernstein's landmark opera recordings.
