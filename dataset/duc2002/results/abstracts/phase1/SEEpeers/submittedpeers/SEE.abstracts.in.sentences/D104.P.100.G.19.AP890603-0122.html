[1] Here is a chronology of events that began Saturday 05/27/1989 in Tiananmen Square: At 1 a.m. tens of thousands of troops enter Beijing marching toward Tiananmen Square, China's symbolic political heart. [2] At 12 noon two truckloads of People's Armed Police attack crowds outside Beijing Hotel. [3] People's Armed Police fire tear gas and use clubs to beat protesters. [4] >From 9 p.m. to 10 p.m. troops hurl tear gas then open fire on the crowds. [5] At 12 midnight armored vehicles break through barricades and trucks carrying military personnel slowly advance toward the square. [6] At 2 a.m. some of the riflemen open fire.
