[1] THE STORY OF SAUD [2] Sheik Saud al Nasir al Sabah said in an interview that he had spoken by telephone to several Kuwaitis taking refuge in Saudi Arabia and Bahrain, who reported that the Iraqis had made it easy for them to leave. That is because the fleeing Kuwaitis apparently are being replaced by Iraqis seeking to meld in with the local population, Sheik Saud al Nasir al Sabah contended . Kuwaiti officials who have been detained by the Iraqi military and who have refused to cooperate have been executed, Saud claimed, adding: "We have eyewitness reports" .
