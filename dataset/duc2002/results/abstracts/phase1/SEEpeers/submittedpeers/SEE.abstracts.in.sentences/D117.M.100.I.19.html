[4] 1989's Booker winner was Kazuo Ishiguro for his novel, ``The Remains of the Day''. [5] Critic and novelist A.S. Byatt on 10/12/1990 won the Booker Prize. [6] The judges awarded the prize to Antonia Byatt for ``Possession,''. [7] The Booker Prize for fiction on 10/11/1994 went to James Kelman for How Late It Was, How Late.
