[1] Erich Honecker Dies in Chile 29 May AU2905165694 Hamburg DPA in German 1602 GMT 29 May 94 -- FOR OFFICIAL USE ONLY Erich Honecker, the former GDR head of state, died at his house in Santiago, Chile on Sunday morning [29 May], according to his lawyer. [2] Lawyer Nicolas Becker, who had represented 81-year-old Honecker before the Berlin court in 1992 and early 1993, told DPA on Sunday afternoon that Honecker had rejected an operation. [3] His colleague, Wolfgang Ziegler, had said earlier that the former most powerful man in the GDR had obviously died from his liver cancer.
