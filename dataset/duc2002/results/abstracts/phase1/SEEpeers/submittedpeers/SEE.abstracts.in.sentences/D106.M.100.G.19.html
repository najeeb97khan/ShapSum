[1] 1944: Mandela joins the African National Congress at age 26. [2] 1962: In August he is charged with incitement and leaving the country illegally; gets five-year jail term. [3] July, 1963: Mandela, seven others are charged with sabotage and conspiracy to overthrow the government. [4] April-June, 1964: Mandela and seven others are sentenced to life in prison. [5] February, 1990: De Klerk lifts ANC ban and says Mandela will be released. [6] On 02/11/1990, the nation's best-known black leader becomes a free man at last. [7] Before he was imprisoned, Mandela was an attorney and an activist who worked against apartheid.
