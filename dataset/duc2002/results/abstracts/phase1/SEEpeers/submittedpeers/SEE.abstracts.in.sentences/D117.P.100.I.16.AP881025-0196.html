[1] Australian novelist Peter Carey was awarded the coveted Booker Prize for fiction Tuesday night for his love story, ``Oscar and Lucinda''. [2] Carey, who lives in Sydney with his wife and son, said in a brief speech that like the other five finalists he had been asked to attend with a short speech in his pocket in case he won.
