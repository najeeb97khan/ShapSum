[1] US CITIES along the Gulf of Mexico from Alabama to eastern Texas were on storm watch on 08/24/1992 as Hurricane Andrew headed west after sweeping across southern Florida. [2] HURRICANE Andrew, claimed to be the costliest natural disaster in US history, on 08/26/1992 smashed its way through the state of Louisiana, but narrowly missing the low-lying city of New Orleans. [3] Initial reports said at least one person had died, 75 been injured and thousands made homeless along the Louisiana coast, after 14 confirmed deaths in Florida and three in the Bahamas. [4] More than Dollars 15bn of damage may have been caused.
