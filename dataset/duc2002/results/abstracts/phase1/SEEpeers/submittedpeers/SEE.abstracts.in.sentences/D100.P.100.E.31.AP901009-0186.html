[1] John Lennon's worldwide message of peace was delivered Tuesday as his song ``Imagine'' was played simultaneously for 1 billion people in 130 countries to celebrate what would have been his 50th birthday. [2] On the West Coast, about 50 people - part of an estimated 1 billion people worldwide who had access to the event - stood near Lennon's star on the Walk of Fame and listened to the ``Imagine'' broadcast: ``Imagine all the people, living life in peace ``You may say I'm a dreamer, but I'm not the only one ``I hope some day you'll join us ``And the world will live as one''. [3] U.S. Armed Forces television and radio beamed it to 80-plus other countries.
