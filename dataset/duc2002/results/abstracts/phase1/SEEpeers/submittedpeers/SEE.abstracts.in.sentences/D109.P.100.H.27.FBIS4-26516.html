[1] BFN According to the latest information offered by the Provincial Agricultural Bureau, a total area of 2.465 million mu of crops have been hit by the flood and of which, 446 000 mu of crops will have no yield, 1.957 million mu of grain have been heavily hit and some 300 million kilogram grain are expected to be lost. [2] Among nine cities and prefectures in Fujian, those seriously hit by the flood are Sanming, Nanping, Ningde, Fuzhou, Quanzhou and Putian. [3] In May, the city was hit by the heavy rainstorm and had big losses and now it suffers again from the flood with some 600 000 mu of crops damaged by flood and 200 million kilogram grain expected to be lost.
