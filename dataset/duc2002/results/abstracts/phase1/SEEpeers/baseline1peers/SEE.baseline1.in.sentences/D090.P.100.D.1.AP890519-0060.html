[1] President Corazon Aquino said today she will not allow Ferdinand Marcos to come home to die and her government will not permit the ousted president to be buried on Philippine soil. [2] Marcos' wife Imelda appealed today to Mrs. Aquino to ``open her heart'' and allow the former president to return. [3] Marcos' lawyer also asked the Philippine Supreme Court to order the return, pledging the ousted leader would cooperate with the Aquino administration. [4] Marcos was reported in critical condition today in a Honolulu hospital after undergoing emergency kidney surgery. [5] Hospital officials said the 71-year-old former Philippine leader was suffering from renal, cardiac and pulmonary failure.
