[1] Dave Johnson of the United States clinched the decathlon gold medal in the final event at the World University Games Sunday . [2] Johnson trailed Mikhail Medved of the Soviet Union by 11 points before the 1,500-meter race, the final event of the two-day competition . [3] "It's the hardest I've ever worked in my life," said Johnson, of Montclair, Calif. [4] "I was scared to death. [5] I knew I had to run at my best or even better to win" . [6] Johnson posted a time of 4 minutes 32.72 seconds in the 1,500. [7] That gave him 727 points for a winning total of 8,216, 154 more than Medved .
