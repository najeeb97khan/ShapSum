[1] EAST DENNIS, Mass. -- It's 5:30 a.m., an icy rain is falling from an indigo sky, and there's a stiff northeaster. [2] This Cape Cod country road is deserted, but for the sweaty silhouette of John Kelley. [3] Bounding up a hill in his thin green Windbreaker, with sand from the beach blowing into his face, Mr. Kelley shouts over the howling gale: "This isn't too bad, you know," he says, though the wind chill factor is about 20 below . [4] [5] Mr. Kelley is in training for the Boston Marathon -- his 60th Boston Marathon.
