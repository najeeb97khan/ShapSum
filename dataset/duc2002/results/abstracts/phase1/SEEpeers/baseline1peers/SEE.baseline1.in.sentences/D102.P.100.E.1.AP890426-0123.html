[1] Here are highlights of Lucille Ball's career: 1911 _ Born Aug. 6, 1911, in Jamestown N.Y., the daughter of an electrician and concert pianist Desiree Ball. [2] 1926 _ Begins career in show business in New York as a chorus girl. [3] 1933 _ Goes to Hollywood as movie showgirl for RKO, later becoming a successful actress appearing in more than 75 films. [4] 1940 _ Marries Desi Arnaz, a Cuban percussionist and bandleader when both were making the movie ``Too Many Girls''. [5] 1951 _ They form Desilu Productions with $5,000 and ``I Love Lucy'' begins its historic six-year domination of television comedy.
