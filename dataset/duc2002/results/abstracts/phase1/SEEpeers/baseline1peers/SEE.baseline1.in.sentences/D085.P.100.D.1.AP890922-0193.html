[1] Here is a chronology of Hurricane Hugo's destructive path across the Caribbean into South Carolina: [2] [3] Sept. 11 _ Forecasters monitor new tropical wave forming off West Africa, southwest of the Cape Verde Islands. [4] Sept. 12 _ Storm reaches the 39 mph tropical storm threshold and is named Hugo. [5] Sept. 13 _ Hugo develops into a hurricane as winds reach 74 mph. [6] Storm is about 1,000 miles east of the Lesser Antilles island chain. [7] Sept. 14 _ Winds increase to 115 mph; storm is 650 miles east of Lesser Antilles. [8] Sept. 15 _ Winds reach 150 mph. [9] Storm, designated a Category 4 hurricane, is considered ``extremely dangerous''.
