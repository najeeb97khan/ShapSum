[1] Rains from Hurricane Gilbert sent a river raging over its banks in Monterrey, sweeping at least 10 policemen to their deaths and overturning buses loaded with evacuees from Matamoros, police said today. [2] The flooding occurred in Monterrey, an industrial city of 3 million people, located 110 miles from the Texas border and 140 miles inland from Matamoros. [3] At least 10 police officers were swept away while on a rescue mission around midnight, a judicial police officer said. [4] Several buses loaded with evacuees overturned in the raging water and more were feared drowned. [5] The officer, who spoke on condition of anonymity, said early today it had been raining for more than 24 hours.
