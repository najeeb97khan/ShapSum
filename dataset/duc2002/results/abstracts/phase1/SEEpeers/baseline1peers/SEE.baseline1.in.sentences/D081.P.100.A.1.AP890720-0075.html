[1] Strikes by tens of thousands of miners in this Siberian coal region spread to four more of the Soviet Union's coal fields, Tass reported today. [2] The official news agency said coal miners in Vorkuta in the far north, the Don River city of Rostov in southwestern Russia and the Ukrainian industrial center of Dnepropetrovsk walked out Wednesday. [3] Miners in the Karaganda region in Soviet Central Asia today refused to work, Tass said. [4] By evening, 14 mines in the region, home of the country's third-largest coal fields, were reported at a standstill.
