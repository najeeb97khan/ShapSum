[1] In a dramatic finish, the San Francisco 49ers football team won the Super Bowl on Sunday with a 20-16 victory over the Cincinnati Bengals. [2] Joe Montana's 10-yard touchdown pass to John Taylor with 34 seconds left provided the winning margin. [3] The victory was achieved through the brilliance of Montana and Jerry Rice, the wide receiver who caught 12 passes for 222 yards, both Super Bowl records. [4] Rice was named the game's most valuable player. [5] The winning score came at the end of a 92-yard drive, engineered by Montana, whose touchdown pass gave him a Super Bowl record with 357 yards passing.
