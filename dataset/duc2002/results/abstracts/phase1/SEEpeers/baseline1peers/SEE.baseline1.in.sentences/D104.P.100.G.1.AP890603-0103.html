[1] Chinese troops stormed Tiananmen Square early Sunday, shooting and beating their way through crowds of pro-democracy protesters in clashes that witnesses and doctors said killed at least 32 people. [2] Hundreds were wounded. [3] The troops cut a violent swath through the heart of Beijing to get to the the square, rolling through barricades and surging masses on the surrounding streets. [4] By 3:30 a.m., soldiers and riot police had surrounded Tiananmen, boxing off several thousand students and supporters concentrated around a monument to revolutionary heroes in the center. [5] A half hour later, lights were turned out. [6] The square was in darkness. [7] ``You have to give up all hope,'' students said over their loudspeaker at the monument.
