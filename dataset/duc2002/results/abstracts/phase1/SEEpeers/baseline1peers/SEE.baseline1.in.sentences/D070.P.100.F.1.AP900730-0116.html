[1] East Germany's deposed Communist leader Erich Honecker is too sick to be held in jail but is fit enough to be tried, the official news agency ADN reported Monday. [2] ADN, quoting a Health Ministry statement, said Honecker was still recovering from surgery for kidney cancer in January and was not well enough to be incarcerated. [3] But a medical exam by a team of doctors found him in condition to be questioned and to stand trial, ADN said.
