[1] The shortlist of six for the #20,000 Booker Prize for fiction prompted the publishing industry to comment [2] that some on the list "are B-team writers at best" and that others "don't make the grade". This is surprising commentary given [3] it is a bumper year for new fiction fulfilling the criteria -English language and non-American. [4] There was some traditional controversy when it was revealed that an author [5] whose novel had made the longlist was actually the wife of a Booker judge. [6] But critics say it's the final list, not the controversy, [7] which must have a final death blow to the Booker.
