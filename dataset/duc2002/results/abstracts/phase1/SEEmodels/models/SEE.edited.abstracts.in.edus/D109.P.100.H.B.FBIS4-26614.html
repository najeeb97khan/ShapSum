[1] This morning Provincial Party Committee Secretary Wang Molin, provincial Governor Chen Bangzhu, and other party and government leaders arrived at Changsha City [2] to direct the fight against the flooding. [3] Governor Chen Bangzhu said in an interview [4] that this flood in Hunan province is of a severity unseen for the past 100 years in terms of both water level and quantity. [5] He said [6] that efforts by Army personnel and the people have protected key dams, [7] thereby keeping economic losses to a minimum.
