[1] President Aquino used small bombers and artillery to end a rebellion [2] led by renegade army Col. Alexander Noble on Mindanao, [3] countering charges [4] that her government is indecisive. [5] The Oct. 4-6 uprising never spread to the military underground on other islands. [6] Aquino has survived two major coup attempts and several aborted military mutinies. [7] Most ended in standoffs [8] or dragged on for days [9] because military leaders were reluctant to order troops to fire on other soldiers. [10] Noble's rebellion coincided with the release of a government report that concluded [11] coup attempts will probably continue [12] - but unlikely to succeed [13] - for the indefinite future.
