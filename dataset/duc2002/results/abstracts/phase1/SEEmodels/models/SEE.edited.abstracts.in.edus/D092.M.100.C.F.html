[1] The largest earthquake [2] (magnitude 7.7) [3] ever to strike Iran occurred on June 20th. [4] The Islamic Republic put the death toll at 36,000 and the number homeless at 400,000. [5] The opposition People's Mojahedin blamed the government for shoddy housing [6] that caused the deaths, which they said were underreported. [7] The government, in turn, blamed the US [8] for robbing it of oil resources [9] and leaving too little for developing modern construction methods. [10] Iran said it would accept aid from all countries, even the US, [11] but not from Israel and South Africa. [12] Relief supplies poured into Iran from all over the world, including the US.
