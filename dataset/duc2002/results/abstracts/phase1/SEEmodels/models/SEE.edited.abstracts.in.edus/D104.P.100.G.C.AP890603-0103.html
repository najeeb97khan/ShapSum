[1] Chinese troops stormed Tiananmen Square early Sunday. [2] The troops cut a bloody swath through the heart of Beijing to reach the square. [3] Many died when troops began firing on street people. [4] By 3:30 A.M. soldiers and riot police had surrounded Tiananmen, [5] isolating thousands of demonstrators concentrated around the Monument to Revolutionary Heroes. [6] Sporadic gunfire continued on the outskirts of the square, [7] and ambulances and pedicabs hauled away the wounded. [8] Troops started moving toward the monument, [9] forcing the students to leave and end a three-week sit-in.
