[2] Lennon had been in seclusion for five years, [3] but in 1980 felt inspired to make an album with his wife Yoko Ono. [4] Newmark spent a month in the studio playing the songs [5] that eventually ended up on "Double Fantasy" and "Milk and Honey", Lennon's final albums. [6] It was Newmark said, the highlight of my career. [7] Prior to meeting Lennon, Newmark was nervous about working with him, [8] but found that Lennon was just a "working musician".
