[1] A Florida couple survived for 66 days at sea in a rubber lifeboat [2] after whales sank their pleasure boat. [3] As the boat was sinking, [4] they grabbed fishing rods and a salt-water purifier [5] and stayed alive by eating raw fish [6] and drinking purified water. [7] William and Simone Butler lost about 50 pounds each [8] and were weak but recovering at a hospital in Costa Rica. [9] A Costa Rican Coast Guard ship rescued the couple [10] who said several whales attacked and sank their boat. [11] A US Coast Guard spokesman said [12] that boats sometimes run into marine mammals [13] but knew of no recorded cases of whale aggression.
