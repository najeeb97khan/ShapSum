[1] By dawn on Sunday tanks and riflemen controlled Tiananmen Square. [2] Troops were deployed on the Monument to Revolutionary Heroes in the square's center, and more at important nearby intersections. [3] Most of the killed late Saturday and early Sunday fell in the streets leading to the square [4] where crowds tried to prevent troops from reaching the square. [5] Once the troops controlled the square and the firing diminished, [6] loudspeakers blared government and student announcements. [7] At 4 A.M. lampposts in the square darkened. [8] After the lights came back on students began filing our of the square.
