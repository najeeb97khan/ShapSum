[1] Ten years after his death, Yoko Ono, wife of former Beatle John Lennon is thriving. [2] The Whitney Museum of Art has exhibited her artwork, [3] the American Film Festival is staging an international tour of her films and a new album is soon to be released. [4] She also founded Bag One Arts, [5] to promote Lennon's art and image. [6] Ono, 57, began [7] her art career as an abstract impressionist, [8] but moved to avant-garde. [9] She met Lennon in 1966 and many have accused her [10] of breaking up the Beatles. [11] Ono was born in Tokyo [12] and moved to the United States in 1936.
