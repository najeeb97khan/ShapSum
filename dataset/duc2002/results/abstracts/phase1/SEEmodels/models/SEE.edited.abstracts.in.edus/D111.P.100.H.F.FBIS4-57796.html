[1] An Argentine corvette carried out control operations against a Korean vessel illegally fishing in Argentine waters. [2] The Foreign Ministry approved the intervention as being in accordance with priority [3] to be given to preserve marine life resources and punishing violators of national laws. [4] The Korean crew sank its own ship [5] after it failed to answer the Argentine ship's warnings. [6] Another Korean ship nearby rescued the crew of the sinking ship.
