[1] Celebrity biographer Albert Goldman had a mythic view of "Lennon the Great" [2] when he began his book The Lives of John Lennon. [3] He called Lennon's widow Yoko Ono for assistance, [4] but she never responded. [5] Goldman says his view of Lennon changed [6] after 1,200 interviews [7] uncovered the worst regarding Lennon. [8] He claimed that Lennon stayed in bed for days at a time, [9] was bisexual, [10] a drug abuser, anorexic, reclusive, [11] and had wild drinking binges. [12] Goldman had also written tell-all biographies of Elvis Presley and Lenny Bruce. [13] Goldman said [14] his books were bad for Yoko business and for Paul McCartney's business.
