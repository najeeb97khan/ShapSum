[1] Hurricane Hugo started as a tropical wave off Africa on Sept. 11th, [2] was named tropical storm Hugo with 39mph winds on Sep 12th, [3] and became a hurricane with 74mph winds on Sep 13th. [4] On Sep 15th it was designated Category 4 with 150mph winds. [5] On Sep 17th it damaged Guadeloupe, Montserrat, Antigua, St. Kitts, and the Virgin Islands, [6] where St. Thomas and St. Croix were worst hit. [7] On Sep 18th it damaged Puerto Rico. [8] Winds slowed to 105 mph then returned to 135mph and Category 4 designation on the 21st, [9] when it hit Charleston. [10] It continued inland with diminishing winds on the 22nd [11] and was downgraded to a tropical storm.
