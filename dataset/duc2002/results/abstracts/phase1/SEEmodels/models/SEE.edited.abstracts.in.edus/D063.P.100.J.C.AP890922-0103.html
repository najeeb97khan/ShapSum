[1] An explosion this morning destroyed a military barracks [2] that housed the Royal Marines School of Music. [3] The School is located in the southern England town of Deal, [4] and police there said the explosion killed 11 and injured 22. [5] The Irish Republican Army claimed responsibility for the explosion. [6] The blast occurred [7] while the school's neighbors were still in bed, or at home preparing for the day's activities. [8] Some homes in Deal were badly damaged. [9] Neighbors described the horror and confusion that followed the explosion, [10] and likened it to those that occurred during the Blitz.
