[1] The owners of Cory, a German short-haired pointer are embroiled in a legal battle over whether or not he should be retired. [2] Cory has won 245 show awards [3] as Best of Breed and 19 awards as Best in Show. [4] One owner, Anthony Attalla is suing the other two, John and Rita Renmondi, [5] charging that they have been showing Cory [6] even though he is officially retired. [7] Attalla claims [8] that as he lays out fees and other expenses, [9] it is his decision [10] when to retire Cory. [11] The Renmondi's claim [12] that Attalla has breached the contract [13] by not paying certain expenses. [14] Stud fees are also at issue.
