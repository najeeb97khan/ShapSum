[1] A biography [2] that portrays former Beatle John Lennon [3] as a drug addled, anorexic, [4] bisexual [5] who raged his way from Liverpool to New York is totally fiction said his widow, Yoko Ono. [6] The book also portrays Ono as a gold-digger who snorted heroin [7] and was responsible for Paul McCartney's 1980 marijuana bust in Japan. [8] Ono admitted having a heroin problem in the late 1970's, [9] but denied that Lennon was homosexual [10] or that he was using drugs [11] while living with his son Sean. [12] She also denied [13] that he had become a recluse. [14] Goldman who conducted more than 1200 interviews for the book has written other controversial biographies.
