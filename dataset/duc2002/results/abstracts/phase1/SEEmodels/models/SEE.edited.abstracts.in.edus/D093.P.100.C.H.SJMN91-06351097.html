[3] Some of the 178 survivors said the crew abandoned them. [4] The 1,105-ton Egyptian-registered ferry, [5] completing a 36-hour trip from Jiddah, [6] went down six miles off the Egyptian coast after inexplicably leaving standard navigational channels [7] and ramming a coral reef in stormy 10-foot seas. [8] According to press, the vessel was taking a shortcut. [9] Authorities warned it back on course [10] but winds sent it out of control. [11] All but 10 passengers were Egyptians, [12] many Muslims returning from pilgrimages to Mecca or guest workers returning from Saudi Arabia.
