[1] The Tatars of the Crimea and the ethnic Germans [2] who lived along the Volga were forcibly resettled by Stalin. [3] They were sent to Siberia and Central Asia, [4] and now they want to return to their traditional homeland. [5] The government has decided to give each of each of the groups an autonomous region in their homeland, but it will be years [6] before they can be relocated. The Russians [7] who have settled in those areas, [8] and wish to remain there must be accommodated [9] before the Tatars and the Germans can return to the Volga.
