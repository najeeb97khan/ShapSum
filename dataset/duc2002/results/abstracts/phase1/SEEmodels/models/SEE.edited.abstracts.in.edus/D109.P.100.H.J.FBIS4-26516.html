[1] According to the Provincial Agricultural Bureau, [2] Fujian suffered major losses due to flooding. [3] Of the 2.465 million mu of crops affected by the flood, [4] 446,000 mu will have no yield and 1.957 million mu of grain have been heavily hit. [5] Sanming, Nanping, Ningde, Fuzhou, and Quanzhou were seriously hit by flooding, with Sanming being the most devastated. [6] In May, a heavy rainstorm caused major losses there. [7] With the recent flooding, [8] an additional 600,000 mu of crops were damaged [9] and 200 million kilogram of grain is expected to be lost. [10] The floods have exacerbated the typically low grain production situation in Fujian.
