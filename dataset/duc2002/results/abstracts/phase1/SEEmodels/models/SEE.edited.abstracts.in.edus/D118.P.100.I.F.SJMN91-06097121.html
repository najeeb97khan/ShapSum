[1] Former Senator John Tower of Texas and his daughter were among 23 people killed in a commuter plane crash. [2] Tower was en route from Atlanta to Sea Island, off the Georgia coast [3] to attend a party in his honor. [4] Just the day before, Senator John Heinz of Pennsylvania and his daughter were killed in a plane-helicopter collision [5] that involved the same type aircraft that Tower was in. [6] Officials did not know the cause of the crash of Tower's plane. [7] Statements of loss came from Senator Lloyd Bentsen of Texas and Senator Sam Nunn of Georgia. [8] Both used the word patriot.
