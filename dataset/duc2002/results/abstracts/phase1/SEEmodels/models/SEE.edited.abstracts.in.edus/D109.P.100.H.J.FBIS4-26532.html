[1] After inspecting Zhuzhou and Xiangtan flood areas, [2] Comrade Wen Jiabao (CPC Central Committee Secretariat member) [3] commended the work in the two cities [4] and issued important instructions. [5] Under the leadership of the provincial party committee and government, the cadres and people had rescued a number of industrial and mining enterprises. [6] After the water recedes, [7] they should concentrate on aiding the victims, [8] resuming production and reconstructing homes. [9] He noted [10] that the peak flood season has not arrived, [11] and the people cannot relax. [12] He also stressed [13] that the current flooding has revealed problems in the construction of water conservancy facilities and urban flood control.
