[1] A team [2] led by Wen Jiabao, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and member of the CPC Central Committee Secretariat, [3] arrived in Jiangxi [4] to provide on-the-spot guidance for the on-going flood fighting [5] and relief work and to direct the resumption of industrial and agricultural production. [6] Wen extended appreciation to everyone [7] who has been doing this emergency work [8] and affirmed their preliminary victories. [9] He emphasized [10] that measures should be taken to salvage the early-ripening rice and to expand the planting of late-ripening rice and autumn grain in order to attain a bumper crop for the entire year.
