[1] More than a dozen bombs exploded yesterday afternoon in Bombay, [2] killing about 200 [3] and injuring more than a 1,000. [4] The devices, mostly car bombs, [5] appeared to be a systematic attack on India's commercial heart. [6] Targets included the Bombay Stock Exchange, Air India building, a shopping complex, and two hotels near the airport. [7] The identity of the bombers is unknown, [8] but the goal appears to be renewing communal strife [9] and undermining government efforts [10] to attract foreign investment and tourism. [11] Leaders have called for calm. [12] All roads into Bombay are blocked [13] and the army is on alert.
