[1] A State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters spokesmen said today [2] that floods along the Xijiang, Beijiang, Xiangjiang and Ganjiang have receded, except for a section of the Xijiang in Wuzhou. [3] Floods hit Guangdong and several other provinces following heavy June storms. [4] The provincial government yesterday urged localities [5] to assist victims [6] and has allocated grain, cash reserves and farm production supplies to the flood-stricken areas. [7] Additionally, 790 million yuan in loans and donations were sent to the disaster area. [8] CPC Central Committee Secretariat member Wen Jiabao inspected flood areas [9] and stated production should be promptly resumed to mitigate economic losses.
