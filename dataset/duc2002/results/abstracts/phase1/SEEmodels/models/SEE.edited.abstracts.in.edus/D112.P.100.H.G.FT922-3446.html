[1] Two Swiss trusts, a private UK trust company and a US investment bank are under investigation by the Serious Fraud Office (SFO) [2] for involvement in an illegal scheme to support the Maxwell Communication Corporation (MCC) share price. [3] Robert Maxwell and his son Kevin, the owners of MCC, are being investigated by SFO for failure [4] to disclose purchases of shares by Swiss Trusts as required by company law. [5] A directive of a secretive Swiss trust said the purchases, [6] using brokers, had been "going on for some time" [7] and were bought for other "foundations" and then held for a certain time.
