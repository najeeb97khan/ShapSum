[1] Thursday night about 30 members of the Filipino-American Movement for a Better Philippines held a candlelight vigil outside the Philippine Consulate in Los Angeles in support of the military coup attempt unfolding in their homeland. [2] Some of the protesters entered the consulate offices [3] and confronted a coalition of four Los Angeles, pro-Aquino Filipino groups holding a press conference. [4] After some minutes of shouting between the Aquino supporters and the anti-government protesters, [5] the protesters departed and order was restored. [6] The coup attempt is being led by officers linked to renegade Lt. Col. Gregorio Honasan. [7] Present at the LA press conference was Andrea Aquino, niece of assassinated Marcos opponent Benigno Aquino, Cory Aquino's husband.
