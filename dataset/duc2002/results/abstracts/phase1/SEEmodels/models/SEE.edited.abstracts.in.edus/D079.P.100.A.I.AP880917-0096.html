[1] Hurricane Gilbert brought more than 24 hours of heavy rain to Monterrey and Matamoros, Mexico, [2] causing severe flooding and deaths. [3] In the industrial city of Monterrey, at least 10 police officers on a rescue mission were swept away by the Santa Catarina River. [4] Several buses full of evacuees were overturned by high water in Matamoros. [5] Heavy rainfall, flooding, mudslides, and tornadoes are accompanying the remnants of Gilbert. [6] Power outages are continuing [7] and many remain in shelters. [8] The hurricane has killed at least 109 people and relief operations [9] to bring emergency food, water, and medical supplies are continuing across the Yucatan Peninsula.
