[1] An explosion at the southern England town of Deal leveled a two-story barracks at the Royal Marines School of Music and seriously damaged many homes in the neighborhood. [2] The band was playing at the time of the explosion [3] and was trapped under the wreckage. [4] The blast, an act of the Irish Republican Army, left 11 dead and 22 injured. [5] Terrified neighbors, some of whom said the blast reminded them of the Blitz, [6] were just starting their day [7] when the explosion occurred. [8] Homes were wrecked as roofs were torn off, [9] ceilings tumbled down [10] and windows were blown out.
