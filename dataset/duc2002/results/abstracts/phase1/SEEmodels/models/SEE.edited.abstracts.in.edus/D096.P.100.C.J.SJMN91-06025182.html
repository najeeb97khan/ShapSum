[1] This is a viewer's guide to Super Bowl XXV. [2] It will be played at Tampa Stadium, Tampa Fla. [3] Winners will get $36,000 per man; [4] losers get $18,000 per man. [5] If the game is tied at the end of regulation time, 60 minutes, [6] it will continue in sudden death overtime. [7] The team scoring first will win. [8] The winning team receives permanent possession of the Vince Lombardi Trophy, [9] a sterling silver football mounted in a kicking position [10] that was created by Tiffany [11] and is valued at over $10,000. [12] The late Vince Lombardi coached the two-time Super Bowl champion Green Bay Packers.
