[1] Iraqi troops, led by the elite Republican Guard invaded Kuwait early today [2] as Iraq claimed it was responding to the request from "the interim government of free Kuwait" to defend the revolution and the Kuwaiti people. [3] Kuwaiti forces failed in their attempt to repel the invaders with heavy artillery. [4] The rattle of automatic weapons and explosions in rapid succession rudely awakened residents.
