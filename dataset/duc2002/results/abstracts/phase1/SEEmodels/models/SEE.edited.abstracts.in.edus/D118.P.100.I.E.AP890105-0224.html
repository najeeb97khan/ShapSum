[1] Former Senator John Tower, President Bush's nominee as defense secretary [2] under went surgery [3] to remove a colon polyp on Thursday. [4] Senator Tower was listed in fair condition following the surgery [5] and is expected to make a full recovery. [6] Tower entered the hospital on Wednesday [7] after a colonoscopy revealed the polyp. [8] A preliminary biopsy of the polyp, which was removed during a three hour surgery, showed it to be benign. [9] A rectal polyp was removed during the colonoscopy [10] which showed malignant but well differentiated cells. [11] An announcement on the first polyp was to come from Tower's surgeon on Thursday.
