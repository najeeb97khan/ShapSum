[1] A campaign at Northeastern University [2] to award an honorary degree to South African nationalist leader Nelson Mandela has been successful. [3] It was necessary to change a university policy, [4] which barred honorary degrees for anyone [5] who could not be present at the ceremony. [6] Mandela is imprisoned in South Africa and the white-minority government will free him [7] only if he renounces the use of violence by forces seeking political power for blacks. [8] South African Archbishop Desmond Tutu will accept the degree for Mandela. [9] The only remaining hitch is getting Mandela family approval, which has not yet been received.
