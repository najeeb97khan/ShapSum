[1] The communist world will get its first taste of an American hamburger next week [2] when McDonald's opens its doors in Belgrade, Yugoslavia. [3] Offering Big Macs, fries and other McDonald's specialties, some wonder if McDonald's will be as popular as the local fast- food treat, Pljeskavica. [4] Described as a clash between the Big Mac and the Pljeskavica, [5] the success of McDonald's depends on the acceptance by the Yugoslavs of the Big Mac over the Pljeskavica. [6] The Pljeskavica, made of ground pork and onions, is served on bread and sells for about a dollar. [7] McDonald's is scheduled to open a restaurant in Budapest, Hungary, next year [8] but an agreement to expand into the Soviet Union has yet to be reached.
