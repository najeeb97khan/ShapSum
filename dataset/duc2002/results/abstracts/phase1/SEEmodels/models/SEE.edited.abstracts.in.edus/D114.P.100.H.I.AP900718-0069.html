[1] The Indian government today imposed direct federal rule over the troubled state of Jammu-Kashmir. [2] Government forces have battled Moslem separatists here since January, and now consider the state too unstable for local government. [3] Tuesday, 29 separatists were killed, [4] bringing the death toll to at least 792. [5] Jammu-Kashmir is 65percent Moslem in the 82 percent-Hindu India, [6] and its Moslems want to form an independent nation. [7] India is claiming Pakistan is arming and training the separatists. [8] India and Pakistan have already fought two wars over the area [9] and talks between the two countries began today in Islamabad [10] to try to mend current relations.
