[1] John Lennon, My Brother was the collaboration of Beatles aficionado, Geoffrey Giuliano, and Julia Baird, half-sister of the late John Lennon. [2] Giuliano had wanted to do a book on Lennon [3] while Baird wanted to concentrate on her and John's often maligned mother, also named Julia. [4] The book, which contains many photos, [5] is more of a family album than biography [6] and is a narrow view of Lennon. [7] It is a mishmash of anecdotes [8] but mostly tells of a mother and son [9] who, though living apart had a loving relationship. [10] Former Beatle Paul McCartney blessed the book [11] and wrote a forward for it.
