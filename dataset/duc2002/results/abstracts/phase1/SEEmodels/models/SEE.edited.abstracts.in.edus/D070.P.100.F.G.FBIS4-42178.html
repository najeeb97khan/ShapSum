[1] Erich Honecker, the former GDR head of state, [2] died at his house in Santiago, Chile on Sunday morning May 29, 1994. [3] His lawyer, who represented the 81-year old Honecker before the Berlin court in 1992 and early 1993, [4] said Honecker rejected an operation [5] but could not comment on the exact cause of Honecker's death. [6] A colleague said earlier that he had obviously died from his liver cancer. [7] After release from prison in 1993, Honecker traveled to Santiago [8] to join his daughter [9] who is married to a Chilean. [10] He and his wife had been living in a wealthy Santiago area.
