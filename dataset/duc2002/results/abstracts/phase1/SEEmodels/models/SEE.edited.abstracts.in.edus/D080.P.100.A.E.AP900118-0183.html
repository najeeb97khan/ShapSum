[1] Anger greeted Thursday's acquittals in the McMartin Pre-School molestation case. [2] While the defense claimed a triumph of justice over hysteria and hype, [3] irate parents blamed the prosecution of being inept. [4] Jurors found Raymond Buckey and his mother, Peggy McMartin Buckey, innocent of 52 charges against them. [5] No verdict was reached on 13 other charges and prosecutors must decide if they wish to call a new trial on those charges.
