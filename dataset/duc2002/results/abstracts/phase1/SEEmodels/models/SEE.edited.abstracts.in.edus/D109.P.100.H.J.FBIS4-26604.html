[1] Guangdong Province Governor Zhu Senlin, [2] inspecting flood control and relief work in Qingyuan city yesterday, said the Beijiang [3] and Xijiang rivers were receding from their record levels. [4] He attributed the province's success [5] in combating the flood to the concerted efforts [6] of locally stationed soldiers stationed and residents. [7] More than 200 people died, [8] 189,000 rooms were destroyed, [9] and 1.2 million hectares of crops ruined in flooding due to successive torrential rainstorms in the river valleys this month. [10] The economic losses were set at 10.2 billion yuan. [11] The reservoirs, however, remained intact and Guangzhou, the capital city, [12] and Zhujiang River Delta remained safe.
