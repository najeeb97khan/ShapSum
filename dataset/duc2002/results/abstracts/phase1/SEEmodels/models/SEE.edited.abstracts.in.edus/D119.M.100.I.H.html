[6] Nominees Joseph Zappala (Spain), [7] Marvin Semble (Australia), [8] Della Newman (New Zealand), and Joseph Gildenhorn (Switzerland) [9] were said qualified only by large campaign contributions, and nomination of loudmouth Peter Secchia (Italy) [10] was called a mistake. [11] Other nominations included Shirley Temple Black (Czechoslovakia), [12] Thomas Melady (Vatican), [13] Keith Brown (Denmark), and William Howard Taft IV (NATO).
