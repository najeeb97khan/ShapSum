[1] John Lennon's message of peace was delivered when his song "Imagine" was played simultaneously for one billion people in 130 countries [2] to celebrate what would have been his 50th birthday. [3] At a United Nations ceremony for the event, Lennon's widow, Yoko Ono said [4] that the world is a better place because of John. [5] A tape that the slain former Beatle made years [6] earlier served as an introduction. [7] "We all want peace, [8] whatever sort of job we have" was Lennon's message. [9] Other observances were held from Liverpool to Moscow to Los Angles. [10] More than 1,000 radio stations from 50 countries broadcast the event.
