[1] Floods [2] that hit Guangdong, Guangxi, Hunan, Jiangxi, Fujian and Zhejiang Provinces have all receded. [3] Caused by torrential rains in the Xijiang and Beijiang river valleys, [4] the floods were the worst in 100 years. [5] The Guangdong provincial government urged localities [6] to help victims with food, clothing, shelter and medical care [7] so that no one would die [8] and no epidemic disease would break out. [9] The province has allocated grain, cash and farm production supplies such as fertilizer and diesel fuel to the flood-stricken areas. [10] Central Party member Wen Jiabao said [11] the key [12] to mitigating economic loss is to resume production as soon as possible.
