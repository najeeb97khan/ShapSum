[1] According to the State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters, [2] water levels of the Xi Jiang, Bei Jiang, Xiang Jiang, and Gan Jiang rivers are dropping. [3] The Xi Jiang and Bei Jiang, in Guangdong Province, [4] crested yesterday, the Bei Jiang at a record 10.39 meters. [5] The industrialized Zhujiang Delta, however, is still threatened [6] because it is traversed by a merged Xi Jiang and Bei Jiang. [7] The Xiang Jiang crested on 19 June, [8] but today the water level is still at 25 meters. [9] Dike reinforcement, therefore, must be accelerated. [10] The flood peak in Gan Jiang entered Boyang Lake on 20 June.
