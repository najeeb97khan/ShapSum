[1] Cassettes and film footage collected by Yoko Ono constituted the main elements of the movie Imagine: [2] John Lennon. [3] The items Ono provided were edited to make the late John Lennon the narrator of the much acclaimed film. [4] Despite her intimacy with the material, Ono found it painful to watch the film. [5] The movie captures the music and times [6] in which Lennon lived [7] but doesn't delve deeply into the Beatles' drug use and abusive tempers. [8] Ono says that there is still enough footage for another movie. [9] Also, Lennon had several unpublished songs, [10] never studio recorded, which might become a record or possibly a musical.
