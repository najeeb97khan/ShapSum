[1] Tour companies are organizing international trips for individuals and families [2] who want to incorporate running into their vacations. [3] The tours are geared to those [4] who want to run in a marathon and last year [5] one tour operator took 5,500 people [6] to tours to 20 countries from Bermuda to Moscow. [7] Runners are guaranteed spots in restricted entry races, such as the London Marathon, [8] which is the world's largest. [9] But many marathons also offer shorter races [10] ranging from 5 miles to a half-marathon. Some of the tours also include sightseeing, [11] guided [12] runs and fun runs into the trip.
