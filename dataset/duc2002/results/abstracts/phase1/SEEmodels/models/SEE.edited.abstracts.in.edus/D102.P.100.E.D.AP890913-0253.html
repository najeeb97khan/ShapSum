[1] Bob Hope paid tribute to Lucille Ball, his frequent co-star in a television special, [2] "Bob Hope's Love Affair with Lucille Ball". The first time [3] they performed together was in 1945 on a show [4] called "G.I. Journal". [5] They knew each other before they worked together as Lucy's husband Desi Arnaz was a band leader on Hope's radio show. [6] Lucille Ball and Bob Hope performed together in many television shows and in several movies. [7] There last appearance together was at the Academy Awards show in March 1989. [8] "She was a very funny woman, even in private" Hope said.
