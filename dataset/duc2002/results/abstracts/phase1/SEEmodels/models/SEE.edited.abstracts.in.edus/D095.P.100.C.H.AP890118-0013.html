[1] Patrick E. Purdy, also known as Patrick West, [2] was a blond, blue-eyed, 26-year-old drifter with a long criminal record in the LA area. [3] He lived with an aunt and uncle in Sandy, Oregon, from July to October, [4] then traveled to Texas, Tennessee, Connecticut, and finally Lodi, California, near his hometown of Stockton, looking for work. [5] On Tuesday he opened fire on Cleveland Elementary School in Stockton, [6] killing five children [7] and wounding 29 others and a teacher, [8] before taking his own life. [9] His aunt and grandmother described him as a nice young man [10] but a loner and childhood alcoholic. [11] His father died years ago after being struck by a car.
