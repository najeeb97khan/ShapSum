[1] The McMartin Pre-School child molestation case, the longest and most costly criminal trial in U.S. history, [2] closed with a verdict of not guilty for Ray Buckey and his mother, Peggy McMartin Buckey. [3] The eight man, four woman jury deliberated for nine weeks [4] before acquitting the Buckeys of 52 counts of molestation. [5] A mistrial was declared on 13 other charges on which the jury remained deadlocked. [6] Most of the jurors later said they believed that the children who testified had been molested [7] but that the prosecution had not proven that the Buckeys were responsible. [8] Disappointed parents expressed anger at the legal system and the prosecution.
