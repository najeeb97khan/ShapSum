[1] The Indian government has been fighting Muslim insurgents in the state of Jammu- Kashmir for the last seven months. [2] More than 1,000 people have been killed. [3] India (predominately Hindu) [4] and Pakistan (predominately Islamic) [5] have fought two wars over Kashmir [6] since the nations were established in 1947. [7] Jammu-Kashmir is 62 percent Muslim and the insurgents want an independent nation. [8] Violence is occurring primarily near Srinagar, the capital, and along the border with Pakistan. [9] India claims the insurgents are being trained and armed in Pakistan. [10] Pakistan denies these charges. [11] The two nations recently exchanged fire, [12] but both say there is no cause for concern.
