[1] Hundreds lined up for the long-awaited grand opening of McDonald's on Moscow's Pushkin Square. [2] Musicians played and dancers danced. [3] The world's newest, largest McDonald's then proceeded to ring up 30,000 meals on 27 cash registers, [4] breaking the opening day record for McDonald's worldwide. [5] Generally accustomed to clerks who snarl, [6] Muscovites got a first-hand look at efficient, fast and friendly service. [7] For most customers [8] it was their first experience with a hamburger. [9] Initial reactions were favorable. [10] This is the first of twenty McDonald's planned for the Soviet Union. [11] It brought to 52 the number of countries where McDonald's operates.
