[1] Trustees [2] filed two writs last week in Manhattan for Mirror Group Newspaper Pension Scheme and Maxwell Communications Corporation Works Pension Scheme. [3] The writs allege [4] that Goldman Sachs, the US-based investment bank, [5] assisted in diverting currency from the two Robert Maxwell-controlled pension schemes [6] to ensure its own debts from Maxwell interest [7] would be repaid. [8] The writs center on the purchase of #55 million of MCC shares from the pension schemes of the two Swiss trusts [9] which were transacted through Goldman. [10] Allegedly, Kevin Maxwell told Goldman to remit the proceeds of the sale to a trust Goldman knew to be Maxwell owned.
