[1] Philippine President Corazon Aquino has agreed to allow Ferdinand Marcos to return [2] so that he can be prosecuted for stealing billions of dollars. [3] Manila newspapers reported today, [4] that she told this to lawyers in Switzerland [5] when she visited there. [6] She has refused to allow Marcos, in exile in Hawaii, back into the Philippines, [7] citing security reasons. [8] He was ousted in a 1986 coup [9] and is accused of stealing $10 billion, [10] much of it allegedly deposited in Swiss bank accounts. [11] Although the Swiss government has frozen these funds, [12] Swiss law allows secrecy requirements to be lifted only for a criminal prosecution.
