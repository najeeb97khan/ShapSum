[1] Huntington, Indiana, the home town of VP Dan Quayle is celebrating his life with the "Premiere Dan Quayle Memorabilia Exhibit". Relatives, classmates, and neighbors have donated many items [2] that are in some way associated with the vice president. [3] The exhibit was put together by the Dan Quayle Commemorative Foundation. [4] The group believes that Quayle, [5] who could not attend because of a busy schedule, [6] deserves the tribute. [7] They are also quick to defend their favorite son [8] but don't expect him to receive much "good press". "We want everyone [9] to know that he is a winner, [10] I don't care what the newspapers say [11] " said one of the exhibit organizers.
