[1] The Ostwind, a yacht built for Adolf Hitler, was sunk to commemorate the "Voyage of the Damned" [2] in which a ship carrying over 900 Jews escaping the Holocaust was turned away [3] and forced to return to Europe and to Nazi concentration camps. [4] The ship was intended to become part of a coral reef. [5] However, the barge carrying it dropped it mile from the intended site threatening a delicate coral reef and sea life. [6] Event sponsors blamed the barge captain for the error. [7] The Ostwind must be moved [8] or cut to pieces to avoid becoming a hazard to ships [9] heading to Miami.
