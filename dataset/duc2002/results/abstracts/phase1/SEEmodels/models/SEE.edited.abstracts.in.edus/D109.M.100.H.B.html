[1] Floods [2] being called the worst in 100 years have hit major parts of Guangdong, Guangxi, Hunan, Jiangxi, Fujian and Zhejiang Provinces. [3] They were caused by torrential rains in the Xiangjiang, Xunjiang, Xijiang and Beijiang river valleys. [4] Cities most seriously affected [5] where Changsha, Zhuzhou and Xiangtan in Hunan Province, Wuzhou in Guangxi Province and Sanming in Fujian Province. [6] Central Party official Wen Jiabao visited Changsha, Zhuzhou, Xiangtan and Jiangxi cities [7] to offer appreciation, encouragement and guidance for the ongoing flood fighting and relief work, [8] and to direct the resumption of industrial and agricultural production. [9] He emphasized measures [10] to salvage the rice crop.
