[1] Julia Baird, former Beatle John Lennon's half-sister, has written a memoir about Lennon's younger years. [2] Baird wrote the book in response to the portrayal of John Lennon as moody neglected child. [3] Baird feels [4] that their mother has been maligned [5] and decided to write the book [6] after seeing a television show that presented her mother as an "unfit mother, no mother at all". Baird was born seven years after Lennon [7] and she and her sister grew up with their parents amid an extended family, including brother John. [8] She writes that her mother and Lennon had a loving relationship, [9] even though living apart.
