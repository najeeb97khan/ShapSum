[1] Dave Johnson of the US clinched the decathlon gold medal in the final event of the World University Games. [2] Another American, Sheldon Blockburger, [3] was in third place in the decathlon until that last event [4] but was overtaken by Szabo of Hungary. [5] Medved of the USSR won the silver. [6] Americans won gold and bronze in the men's 400-meter hurdles. [7] Ana Quirot of Cuba beat Jearl Miles of the US in the women's 400 meters. [8] American Llewellyn Starks was third in the long jump. [9] Romanian Paula Ivan set a meet record in winning the women's 3,000-meter race.
