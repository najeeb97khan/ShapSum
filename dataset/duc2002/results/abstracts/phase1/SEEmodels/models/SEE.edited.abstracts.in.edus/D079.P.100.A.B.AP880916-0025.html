[1] Hurricane Gilbert, after devastating Jamaica and then the Yucatan Peninsula, has been downgraded from Category 5 to Category 3 [2] but may again gather strength over water [3] while moving west-northwest toward landfall. [4] A warning has been issued for Mexico's northern coast and the southern half of the 370-mile Texas coast. [5] The Texas Governor dispatched the National Guard [6] and has issued an emergency proclamation "to preserve the health, safety and welfare of the public". Homeowners are boarding up [7] and laying in supplies to protect against this massive hurricane, [8] which, to date, has taken at least 47 lives in the Caribbean [9] and caused over $8 billion in damage.
