[1] Oscar nominations and victories increase film earnings, with a Best Picture award worth about $20 million. [2] Studios go to increased lengths to get votes, with promotional ads, banquets, and gifts. [3] Members working in specific categories make nominations, [4] but all voting members may vote in all categories. [5] Over 4600 members receive ballots. [6] Voting is on the honor system, [7] but there is no guarantee that academy members fill out their own ballots. [8] Some Oscar voting is done by committee, in violation of voting rules, [9] when voters unfamiliar with the candidates seek the consensus of friends. [10] Authenticity is ensured solely by a control number.
