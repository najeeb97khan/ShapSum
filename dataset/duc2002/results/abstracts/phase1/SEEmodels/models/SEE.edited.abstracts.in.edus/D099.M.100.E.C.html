[1] Marathons [2] are contested over a course that measures 26 miles and 385 yards. [3] They are very popular, [4] and most of the world's major cities organize an annual marathon.. Tour companies even put together "marathon tours" to accommodate runners. [5] Most marathons offer substantial prize money to race winners, [6] but most in the throngs of participants run only for a sense of accomplishment. [7] Each city tailors its marathon [8] to provide the best of its natural beauty and history. [9] London has the largest, and Boston the most storied. [10] San Francisco and San Diego boast of their weather conditions.
