[1] Thousands of Moslems gathered for the funeral of Ashfaq Mmajid Wani, a leader of the Jammu-Kashmir Liberation Front separatist group. [2] Police shot him [3] after he tried to hurl a grenade into a police bunker [4] while riding by on a motor scooter.
