[1] The battleship Iowa steamed home from Puerto Rico Friday under its own power, following an explosion on board that killed 47. [2] Investigators interviewed crewmen [3] and reviewed a videotape of the explosion made by an officer to record the firing of the big guns. [4] Reportedly, deaths in the gun turret's upper chamber resulted from the explosion and fire [5] while those in lower levels were from suffocation [6] when the blast sucked all the oxygen out of the spaces. [7] After extinguishing the fire, crew removed shells and powder from two other gun barrels in the turret. [8] Senate Armed Services Committee will hold hearings on the explosion.
