[1] Biographer Edward Behr released information Wednesday about his BBC documentary on Hirohito. [2] President Truman, the show claims, [3] decided not to prosecute Hirohito for war crimes, [4] and Japan's wartime prime minister, Gen. Tojo, was pressured into changing his testimony so the emperor would not be incriminated. [5] The show also challenges the theory [6] that Hirohito was forced to accept his generals' plans, [7] alleging that he knew about the 1937 massacre of over 20,000 Chinese at Nanking [8] and had advance knowledge of the Pearl Harbor attack. [9] BBC will show the documentary on Jan 24. [10] It will air in the United States in March.
