[1] Lucille Ball was born August 6, 1911, in Jamestown, New York. [2] She began, in 1926, as a chorus girl [3] and moved to Hollywood in 1933, [4] appearing in over 75 films. [5] She married Desi Arnaz in 1940 [6] and, in 1951, they formed Desilu Productions [7] and began "I Love Lucy." [8] When Lucy and Desi divorced in 1960, [9] Lucy bought Desi's share of the company [10] and sold it in 1967 for $18 million. [11] In 1961, she married Gary Morton [12] and next year started "The Lucy Show", [13] which became "Here's Lucy" [14] and ended in 1974. [15] She later starred in two movies and another, short-lived, TV show.
