[1] The Hezbul Mujahedeen is believed responsible for kidnapping [2] and killing four Moslems [3] whose bodies were found in the town of Sopore. [4] The Hezbul Mujahedeen is one of more than a dozen Moslem organizations fighting for independence of the Kashmir valley from India. [5] The murdered Moslems were said to be police informants. [6] A militant ambush of an army convoy killed one soldier [7] and wounded three others. [8] These deaths raise the total of those killed since January 20th to 630. [9] Moslem militants had earlier demanded union with Pakistan, but more recently are demanding independence for Kashmir and the status of a neutral nation.
