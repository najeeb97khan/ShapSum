[1] A passenger ferry sank off the coast of Guatemala [2] while being towed to port. [3] Fifty-nine people drowned and 6 are missing. [4] The ferry ran out of fuel mid-way on a regular 16- mile run across Amatique Bay [5] and sank while a tugboat was towing it to shore. [6] A Spanish citizen was reported dead, [7] but his wife and son survived. [8] The victim was working with a Spanish technical aid and economic development mission in Guatemala. [9] The nationality of the other victims was not known. [10] Navy patrol boats, fishing vessels and private citizens have been searching for survivors and recovering bodies.
