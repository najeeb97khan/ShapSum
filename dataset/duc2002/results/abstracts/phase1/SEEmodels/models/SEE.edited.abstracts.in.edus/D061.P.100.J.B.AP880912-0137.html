[1] Hurricane Gilbert slammed into Kingston, Jamaica on Monday, with torrential rains and 115mph winds [2] that ripped roofs off buildings, [3] uprooted trees, [4] downed power lines [5] and did heavy damage to the airport and parked aircraft. [6] No fatalities in this city of 750,000 people have been reported. [7] Jamaica's popular north coast is not expected to receive as much damage as the south coast, [8] where officials urged residents to seek higher ground. [9] The storm skirted Puerto Rico and is now tracking toward the Cayman Islands. [10] The U.S. Navy reports [11] that its Cuban bases at Guantanamo Bay and Roosevelt Island appear to be relatively safe.
