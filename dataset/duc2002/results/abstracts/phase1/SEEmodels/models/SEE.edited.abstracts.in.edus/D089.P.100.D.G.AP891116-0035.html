[1] In Huntsville, AL a tornado slammed into a shopping district, a residential area, and a school Wednesday, killing at least 15 people. [2] 305 people were injured [3] and about 500 displaced. [4] People are still trapped in vehicles, apartment complexes, and shopping areas. [5] The rubble is 10 to 14 feet deep and rescuers are hampered by darkness and plunging temperatures.
