[1] Erich Honecker, the former head of East Germany, [2] died on 29 May 1994 in Santiago Chile. [3] The 81-year-old Honecker and his wife Margot Honecker, had been living with their daughter in Santiago, [4] since his release from prison in 1993. [5] Honecker had been suffering from liver cancer. [6] Honecker's lawyer, Nicolas Becker said [7] that while he had refused an operation, [8] Honecker had done everything sensible in regard to treatment. [9] It is not clear [10] whether or not Margot Honecker will be allowed to remain in Chile. [11] It was not known [12] if Honecker had left a will [13] or where the funeral would be held.
