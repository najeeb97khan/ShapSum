[1] The battle for Tiananmen Square was fought primarily in the streets leading to the square [2] where scores of people fell trying to prevent troops and armored military vehicles from moving on the students camped in the square. [3] The first troops entered the square about 1 a.m. Sunday [4] followed by a huge convoy of armored vehicles that surrounded the few thousand students [5] that remained. [6] A propaganda battle by loudspeaker ensued. [7] By 4:30 a.m. the students decided by voice vote to leave the square to avoid further bloodshed. [8] As dawn broke rifle-toting troops ruled the vast expanse occupied the day before by students seeking a freer society.
