[1] A representative of the African National Congress said [2] the South African government would soon release Nelson Mandela from prison. [3] Mandela, 70, was jailed for life 27 years ago for conspiring to overthrow the government of South Africa. [4] He was recently transferred from prison to hospital for treatment of tuberculosis. [5] The ANC is fighting to end white-minority rule in South Africa. [6] The release may be an attempt [7] to win international support for the South African government. [8] A South African government source indicated [9] the rumors of Mandela's release were orchestrated by the anti-apartheid movement [10] to pressure the government. [11] Apartheid is South Africa's policy of racial separation.
