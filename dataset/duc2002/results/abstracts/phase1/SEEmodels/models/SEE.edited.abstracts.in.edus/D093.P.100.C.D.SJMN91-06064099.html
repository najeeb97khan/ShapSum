[1] A boat carrying hundreds of Somali refugees struck a reef off Kenya [2] and sank, [3] killing more than 130 people. [4] The ferry carried over 600 people. [5] Police, navy divers, tourists and local residents recovered 130 bodies, [6] but 50 victims were still believed trapped in the sunken vessel. [7] It was one of many vessels [8] that brought thousands of Somalis seeking refuge to Kenya since rebels [9] over threw the Somali president. [10] The fighting now appears to have largely stopped, [11] but refugees continue to arrive. [12] Most of those who drowned were on the lower deck. [13] Earlier, 11 Somalis drowned when a boat capsized off the coast of Kenya.
