[1] Conductor Leonard Bernstein has cancelled several performances in Japan due to exhaustion and lingering illness. [2] He will return to the United States [3] to recuperate. [4] His doctor announced he never fully recovered from ailments he suffered in the spring [5] and needs complete rest for an extended period. [6] Bernstein had been scheduled to conduct the London Symphony Orchestra in four performances in Japan [7] and appear with young musicians during the Pacific Music Festival. [8] He inaugurated the festival last month, [9] believed to be the first festival in Asia where young musicians can train with a professional orchestra. [10] Bernstein issued a separate apology to his fans and sponsors.
