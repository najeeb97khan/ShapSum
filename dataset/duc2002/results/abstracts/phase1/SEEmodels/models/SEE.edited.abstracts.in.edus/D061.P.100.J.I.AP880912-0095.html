[1] Hurricane Gilbert hit Jamaica today with 110 mph winds and torrential rain, [2] causing serious damage in Kingston overnight. [3] The storm center is expected to hit land at Clarendon parish, [4] then move lengthwise across the island. [5] The government is preparing for the worst with government agencies on alert and coastal residents directed to move to higher ground. [6] Communications have already been affected. [7] Gilbert, described as one of the larger systems, [8] has already caused some damage in Puerto Rico, the Dominican Republic, Haiti, and Cuba. [9] Fears are high on the Cayman Islands, the next target on its track.
