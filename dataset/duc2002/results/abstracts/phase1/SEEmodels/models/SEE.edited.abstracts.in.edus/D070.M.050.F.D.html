[1] Erich Honecker, former head of East Germany, [2] died in 1994 in Chile. [3] He had lived there since his release from prison in 1993. [4] Honecker was toppled from power in 1989 after 18 years of his oppressive regime. [5] Following his downfall, he was arrested [6] and charged with treason, corruption, abuse of power and manslaughter.
