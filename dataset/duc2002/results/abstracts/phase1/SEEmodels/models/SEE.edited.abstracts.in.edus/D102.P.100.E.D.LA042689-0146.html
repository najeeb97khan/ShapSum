[1] Lucille Ball, 77, died 26 April 1989 at Cedar-Sinai Medical Center a week [2] after undergoing emergency heart surgery. [3] She and her first husband, Desi Arnaz starred in the groundbreaking television show, [4] "I Love Lucy", from 1951 until 1957. [5] The weekly show became one of the most popular in television history. [6] Ms. Ball and Arnaz signed an $8 million two-year contract with CBS in 1953, the largest in television history. [7] Together they formed Desilu, [8] which went on to produce some of the top shows of the '50s and '60s. [9] The couple divorced in 1960. [10] They had two children, [11] both of whom became actors.
