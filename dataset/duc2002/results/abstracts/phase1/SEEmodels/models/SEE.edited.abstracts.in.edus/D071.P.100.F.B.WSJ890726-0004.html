[1] One of the three owners of Cory, a champion German short-haired pointer is suing the other two for damages and to determine [2] if Cory is officially retired. [3] Cory has won 245 shows as Best-of-Breed and 19 awards as Best-in-Show. [4] Plaintiff Attalla claims [5] the owners' agreement specifies [6] that he advances fees to show the dog [7] and he decides [8] when Cory retires. [9] He is suing the other two owners, John and Rita Remondi for $125,000 in fees and to stop them from showing Cory, [10] who has been officially retired as announced in Dog News. [11] The Remondis claim [12] that Cory's retirement is fraudulent.
