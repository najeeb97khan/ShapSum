[1] Exotic Kashmir has become a war zone [2] where Moslem separatists are seeking independence from predominately Hindu India. [3] At least 72 people have been killed since the first of the year. [4] The 24-hour curfew in Srinagar was lifted briefly on Monday so people could shop. [5] Electricity was turned off on Friday, the Moslem Sabbath, [6] to silence the mosques' loudspeakers. [7] The governor of Jammu-Kashmir claims only a "handful of terrorists" are fighting, [8] but others say the situation is critical. [9] Kashmir, predominately Moslem, was divided between India and Moslem Pakistan in 1947. [10] India claims Pakistan is training and arming the separatists; [11] Pakistan denies this.
