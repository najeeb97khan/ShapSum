[1] More than 2,500 dogs, [2] representing 118 breeds, [3] will compete at Sunday's All Breed Dog Show and Obedience Trials at the Los Angeles Sports Arena. [4] They will undergo demonstrations and tests to show their skills. [5] This is a chance for all [6] to learn about the possibilities, advantages and disadvantages of different breeds. [7] Other attractions include an expert [8] who will answer behavioral questions about pets, free veterinary advice, [9] a demonstration of how 4-H youngsters train puppies to become guide dogs and more than 70 vendors selling dog paraphernalia. [10] A computer will enable visitors [11] to determine which breeds would best suit their life styles.
