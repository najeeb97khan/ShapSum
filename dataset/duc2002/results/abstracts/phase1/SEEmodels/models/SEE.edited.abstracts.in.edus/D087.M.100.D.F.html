[1] The US had its worst Winter Olympic showing in 52 years the1988 Games with few medal winners. [2] In the 1988 Summer Games in Seoul, the US faired better [3] but was still outpaced by the Soviets. [4] Those games were marred by drug usage [5] and suffered from poor TV coverage. [7] An earlier Olympic hero, the late Jesse Owens was honored with a Congressional Gold Medal. [8] The more affluent nations continue to dominate in the Olympics, [9] especially in relay events.
