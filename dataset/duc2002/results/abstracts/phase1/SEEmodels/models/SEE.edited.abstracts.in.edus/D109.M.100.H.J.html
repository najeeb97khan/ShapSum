[1] Flooding in Guangdong, Hunan, Jiangxi, Guangxi, Fujiang and Zhejiang provinces [2] that followed after heavy June storms has subsided, [3] but in Guangdong 200 people died and 1.2 million hectares of crops were ruined. [4] Fujiang also suffered substantial agricultural losses. [5] Wen Jiabo (CPC Central Committee Secretariat member) [6] and other officials toured the flooded areas. [7] They praised the soldiers and civilians [8] who battled the floods, [9] saved key infrastructure and industrial resources and assisted victims. [10] The people were urged to concentrate on recovery efforts and actions to minimize economic losses. [11] Resumption of agricultural production in Jiangxi, one of China's major bread baskets, [12] was of particular concern.
