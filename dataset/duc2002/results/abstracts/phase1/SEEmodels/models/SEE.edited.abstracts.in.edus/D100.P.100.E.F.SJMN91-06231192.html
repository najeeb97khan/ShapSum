[1] Yoko Ono, widow of the late Beatle, John Lennon promotes her own works, [2] but her main activity is to promote Lennon's art and image. [3] She and Lennon were married in 1970. [4] Some believe she controlled him [5] but others question if he could be controlled. [6] She is often blamed for the breakup of the Beatles, [7] but that would likely have happened without her. [8] The remaining ex-Beatles were never fond of her. [9] Ono was born in Tokyo [10] but lived in the US from 1936 until WWII, [11] when the family had to return to Japan. [12] She had two other marriages before Lennon. [13] She now lives in New York and Geneva.
