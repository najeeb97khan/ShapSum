[1] Wen Jiabao (alternate member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and CPC Central Committee Secretariat member) [2] accompanied by Mao Zhiyong (secretary of the Jiangxi Provincial Party Committee) [3] and Governor Guanzheng, inspected Jiangxi province flood areas [4] and issued instructions. [5] We Jiabao expressed appreciation to the servicemembers and civilians fighting the floods. [6] He pointed out, however, [7] that while continuing the work in Ganjiang River valleys, [8] flood-prevention in the Poyang Lake area should be stressed. [9] Further, Jiangxi is a major agricultural province. [10] Immediate action should be taken to salvage the early-ripening rice [11] and expand the planting of late-ripening rice and autumn grain.
