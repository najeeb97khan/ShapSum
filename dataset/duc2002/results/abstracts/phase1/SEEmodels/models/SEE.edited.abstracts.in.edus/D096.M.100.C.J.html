[2] Sixty-three percent of TV viewers watched the lopsided game [3] which also featured the conclusion of the "Bud Bowl II," a $7 million Anheuser Bush ad campaign. [4] Though the game can spotlight a community's problems, [5] it also has tremendous economic impact. [9] Even with tougher business entertainment tax laws, companies are well represented at the event. [10] Traditionally, the winning Super Bowl team is invited to the White House.
