[1] Benjamin Martinez of Mexico and Kathy Smith of Newport Beach were crowned winners of the San Diego Marathon, [2] earning $2,000 apiece. [3] Martinez, a relative newcomer to long distance running, [4] finished in 2 hours, 19 minutes and 3 seconds and Smith finished in 2:43:05. [5] Martinez, a world-class biathlete has run only two other marathons. [6] He said he entered the race because he felt the field was weak. [7] Smith was a 5,000 and 10,000-meter specialist at Stanford, [8] but is now focusing on longer distances. [9] Deanna Sodoma of Escondido won he fir marathon wheelchair race in 2:17:52 and Saul Hernandez of Mexico won the men's division in 1:57:50.
