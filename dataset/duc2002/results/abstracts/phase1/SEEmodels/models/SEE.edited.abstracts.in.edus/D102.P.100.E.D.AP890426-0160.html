[1] Lucille Ball was mourned in her hometown of Jamestown New York. [2] The 77-year-old comedian died of a ruptured aorta in Los Angeles 26 April 1989. [3] She was born in Jamestown in 1911 [4] where she showed an early interest in show business. [5] She moved to Hollywood in 1933. [6] Although she had not visited Jamestown since 1956, [7] she regularly donated money to charities, civic events and Jamestown community college. [8] She was to have received an honorary degree from the college in May. [9] The degree will be awarded posthumously. [10] Townspeople remembered her as full of life [11] and expressed sadness at her passing.
