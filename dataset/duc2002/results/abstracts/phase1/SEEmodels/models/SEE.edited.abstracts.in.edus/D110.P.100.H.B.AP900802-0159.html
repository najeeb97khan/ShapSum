[1] An Arab League Council meeting of foreign ministers was convened in Cairo at Kuwait's request. [2] The Council failed to publicly condemn Iraq's invasion of Kuwait. [3] So far, only Lebanon's prime minister and foreign minister, Salim Hoss, has joined Kuwait [4] to publicly condemn this invasion [5] -the first time in modern history [6] one Arab country invaded another to take control of the government.
