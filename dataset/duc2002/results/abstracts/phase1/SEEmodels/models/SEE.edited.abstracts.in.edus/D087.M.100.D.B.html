[1] In the world of international competition, the Olympics are special. [2] It is here that national pride is displayed, [3] individual dreams are realized [4] and fame is attained. [5] The summer Olympics with its gymnastics, track and field, and swimming events will showcase superstars such as Mary Lou Retton, Carl Lewis, Florence Griffith Joyner and Greg Louganis, some of whom capitalize on their fame and glory with lucrative advertising contracts. [6] The Winter Olympics generally highlight the figure skaters, speed skaters and downhill skiers. [7] Sometimes an Olympian acquires lifelong fame, [8] as happened to Jesse Owens, [9] who in 1936 soundly defeated Hitler's "master race" competitors.
