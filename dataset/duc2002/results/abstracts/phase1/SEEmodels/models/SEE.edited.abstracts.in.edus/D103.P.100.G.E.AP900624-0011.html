[1] An editorial in the anti-American newspaper Johamri Islami in Iran stated [2] that the united States is partly to blame for the thousands of deaths in last week's earthquake. [3] Saying that even the people buried in the rubble chant "Death to Americans," [4] the newspaper accused the U.S. of plundering Iran's resources. [5] In doing so, the United States diverted Iran's national wealth from urban development projects and the construction of earthquake resistant buildings. [6] The newspaper called on the U.S. to stop hatching plots [7] and called on a rejection of aid from the U.S. Iran has said [8] it welcomes relief from the American Red Cross and other humanitarian groups.
