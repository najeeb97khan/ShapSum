[1] A violent storm spawned tornadoes and thunderstorms across the Midwest and South, [2] then blew north knocking over a school cafeteria wall in upstate New York, [3] killing seven children. [4] Altogether, fourteen states and Washington D.C. were affected. [5] The toll from the two-day storm is at least 27 dead and over 500 hundred injured. [6] Property damage is in the millions. [7] The worst damage by far was in Huntsville, Alabama [8] where a tornado slammed into a shopping district, a residential area and a school, [9] leaving at least 14 dead and injuring nearly 300. [10] The Governor [11] called in State Troopers and the National Guard to assist.
