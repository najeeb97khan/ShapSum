[1] Iranian editorial writers are rejoicing [2] that the rest of the world is waking up to one of their most hated bogeymen -Saddam Hussein. [3] Saddam attacked Iran in September 1980 and the war dragged on until August 1988. [4] For the last six months, editorial writers have backed off Saddam [5] because of U.N. sponsored bilateral talks. [6] The invasion of Kuwait changed this.
