[1] Emperor Hirohito of Japan has lost 40 pounds in the two months [2] since he fell ill, [3] but his internal bleeding appears to have stopped. [4] The 87-year-old monarch, [5] responds to questions, [6] but is less talkative than before. [7] He has a low-grade fever and doctors have said [8] he suffers from jaundice and anemia. [9] A spokesman said [10] his heart and kidneys are functioning normally. [11] Since September, [12] when he was vomiting blood, [13] Hirohito has received 42 pints of blood. [14] Palace officials have not given details of the Emperor's illness [15] and would not comment on news reports [16] that he has pancreatic cancer.
