[1] A flash flood raged through small town areas along the Ohio River in eastern Ohio. [2] So far, at least ten people are known dead [3] but dozens are missing. [4] The flood created a wall of water 15 to 20 feet high as it tore through the area. [5] The flood struck near the village of Shadyside, Ohio. [6] The village is about ten miles south of Wheeling, West Virginia. [7] A crowded bar in Shadyside was washed away when the torrent of water caved in a wall. [8] Only two people have been accounted for. [9] Ohio Governor Celeste declared a state of emergency [10] after surveying the area.
