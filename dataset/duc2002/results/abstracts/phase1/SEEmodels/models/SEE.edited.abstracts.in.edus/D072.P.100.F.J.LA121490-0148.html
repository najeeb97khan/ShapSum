[1] Thursday, Broadway honored Leonard Bernstein who died on October 14th at 72. [2] A capacity audience at the Majestic Theater heard recollections about the musical genius [3] and listened to classic Bernstein songs. [4] Arthur Laurents, who wrote the book for "West Side Story," hosted. [5] Carol Lawrence, the original Maria of "West Side Story," [6] sang as did cast members from other Bernstein shows, including Nancy Walker, Betty Comden, and Adolph Green [7] ("On the Town") and Barabara Cook ("Candide"). [8] Lauren Bacall, Phyllis Newman, and Kurt Ollman also performed. [9] The program finale, "Somewhere," [10] sung by Marilyn Horne was followed by a sustained standing ovation for "Lenny."
