[1] Though 63% of TV viewers or approximately 109 million people watched Super Bowl XXIV on CBS, [2] the network finished the week in the cellar in the ratings race. [3] The lopsided game was the lowest rated Super Bowl since 1969. [4] Each rating point in 1990, however, represents many more households than it did two decades ago. [5] This marks only the third time [6] that the Super Bowl network did not win the week and only the second time [7] that it was third for the week. [8] On the upside, the game was a good lead-in to the new CBS series, "Grand Slam."
