[1] In the decade since the death of former Beatle John Lennon, his wife, [2] Yoko Ono has issued several albums and collections of his work. [3] Ono says she wants to keep Lennon's spirit alive, [4] but others charge that she has made millions with no concern for his legacy. [5] Ono has designed [6] and sold memorabilia from coffee mugs [7] to lithographs, [8] using Lennon's image. [9] Ono says that some of the money goes to the Spirit Foundation, [10] established by Lennon and her before his death. [11] The foundation has contributed to causes ranging from a home for drug-addicted children to bulletproof vests for police officers.
