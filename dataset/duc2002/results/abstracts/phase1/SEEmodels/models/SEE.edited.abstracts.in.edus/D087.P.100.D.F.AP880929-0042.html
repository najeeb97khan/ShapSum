[1] Florence Griffith Joyner is on target [2] to become only the second woman [3] to win four gold medals in track and field events in a single Olympics. [4] Her gold medals were in the 100 and 200 meter sprints set new Olympic and World records. [5] The flamboyant Griffith Joyner is confident [6] that she can run even faster. [7] Her next chance to win gold would come in the 400 and 1600 meter relays. [8] If successful in those events, [9] she would match the quadruple gold medal performance by American Carl Lewis in 1984 and Fanny Blankers-Koen of the Netherlands in 1948.
