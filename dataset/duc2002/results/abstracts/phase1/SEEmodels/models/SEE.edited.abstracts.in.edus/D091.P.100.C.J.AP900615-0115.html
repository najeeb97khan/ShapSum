[1] Thunderstorms swept through central and eastern Ohio Thursday night causing flooding [2] that killed at least 10 people in Belmont County [3] and caused the evacuation of about 200 residents from their homes in Licking and Jefferson counties. [4] Another 40 to 60 residents of Belmont County are missing. [5] Two creeks outside the Ohio River village Shadyside overflowed [6] when about 4 inches of rain fell in about an hour. [7] Authorities said [8] a "wall of water" was created. [9] Homes were knocked off their foundations and a bar in Shadyside was swept away. [10] Gov. Richard Celeste declared a state of emergency.
