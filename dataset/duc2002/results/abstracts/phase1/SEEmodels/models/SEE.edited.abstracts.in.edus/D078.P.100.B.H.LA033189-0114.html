[1] This year's Academy Awards ceremonies opened with an elaborate, 10-minute Snow White song-and-dance routine, without Disney's permission. [2] The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences told Disney only that the program contained no material deleterious to Disney interests. [3] Disney sued for copyright infringement [4] when the academy refused to publicly apologize for unauthorized use of the Disney character.
