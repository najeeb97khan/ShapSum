[1] The Democrat-controlled Senate rejected the nomination of former Texas Senator John Tower to be Secretary of Defense on a 53-47 vote. [2] The rejection came after a bitter debate [3] that focused on Tower's drinking habits, womanizing, and business dealings with defense contractors. [4] Kansas GOP Senator Nancy Kassebaum was the only Republican [5] to oppose Tower. [6] Three Democrats had voted for Tower. [7] After the voting, Tower called Senator John Warner [8] and asked him to thank those [9] who had supported him. [10] Majority Leader George Mitchell moved to repair relations between the Senate and President that had been damaged during the debate.
