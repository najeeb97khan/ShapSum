[1] According to biographer Albert Goldman in his book The Lives of John Lennon [2] the late, former Beatle spent much of a three-year period sitting in his room in the lotus position and smoking marijuana. [3] Between 1976 and 1979, Goldman writes [4] that Lennon "could not be more remote". Lennon was [5] an anorexic, the eating disorder traced to a comment [6] that he was "the fat Beatle". The book claims [7] that Lennon's second wife, Yoko Ono, had a $5,000 a week heroin habit and that John also took heroin [8] and consumed LSD. [9] Goldman quotes Lennon as saying about LSD "I just ate it -like candy".
