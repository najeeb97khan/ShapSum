[1] On January 16, 1989, Patrick Edward Purdy, 24, fired an AK-47 into a playground at Stockton, California's Cleveland Elementary School, [2] which he had attended. He killed five children [3] and wounded 30, [4] then killed himself. [5] The slain children were Southeast Asian. [6] Purdy had drifted around the country searching for work [7] and had a long criminal record.
