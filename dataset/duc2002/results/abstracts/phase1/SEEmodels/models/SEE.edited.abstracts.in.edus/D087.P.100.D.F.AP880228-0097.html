[1] The US had its worst Winter Olympic Games showing in 52 years, [2] taking only six medals. [3] Two of the medals were gold [4] won by Brian Boitano in figure skating and Bonnie Blair in speed skating. [5] Blair also won a bronze, [6] making her the only US double winner. [7] The US narrowly missed winning a bronze in the bobsled event [8] when they were beaten by only. 02 seconds. The Soviet hockey team had it first loss of the Games at the hands of Finland [9] but still managed to win the gold [10] while Finland won the Silver. [11] Overall, the Soviets won the most medals, [12] followed by East Germany and Switzerland.
