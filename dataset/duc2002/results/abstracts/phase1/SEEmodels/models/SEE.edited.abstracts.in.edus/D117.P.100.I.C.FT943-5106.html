[1] Yesterday' announcement of the short-list of six contenders for the1994 Booker Prize elicited some biting comments. [2] Some of those on the list were characterized as "B-Team Writers at the Best". This is surprising in a year of good works of fiction [3] that meet the criteria for Booker consideration in that [4] they are written in English and are non- American. [5] They are missing from the short-list, making the criticism more strident. [6] Despite the carping, there will be the usual high interest from now until 11 October [7] when the winner is announced.
