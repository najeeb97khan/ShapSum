[1] The World Jewish Congress plans to hold a conference at Wansee [2] where the Nazi leadership met on January 20, 1942 to implement the "final solution" of the Jewish problem. [3] The meeting is scheduled for May 8th, the 45th anniversary of VE day. [4] It will be the first meeting on German soil, [5] and 100 are expected to attend. [6] The President of the organization says [7] that Jews must make a moral statement on German reunification, [8] and Wansee is the right place to make it. [9] Part of the statement is [10] that the Holocaust must never be repeated.
