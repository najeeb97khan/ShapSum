[1] The nonpartisan American Academy of Diplomacy rated Joseph Gildenhorn, Bush's nominee for US ambassador to Switzerland, [2] unqualified based on a comparison of his biography and the job's requirements. [3] Gildenhorn and his family have given $230,000 to Republican campaigns. [4] Twenty of Bush's first 29 ambassadorships have gone to large GOP contributors. [5] Democrats complain [6] that choice diplomatic posts are better reserved for Foreign Service professionals. [7] Gildenhorn, 59, is a lawyer, real estate developer, and vice-chairman of the board of directors of the Sovran Bank. [8] Other ambassadorial nominees rated as unqualified include Joseph Zappala (Spain), [9] Melvin Semble (Australia), and Della Newman (New Zealand). [10] All are in real estate.
