[1] Lucille Ball, star one of the most popular shows in the history of television "I Love Lucy", [2] died at the age of 77 in April 1989. [3] Miss Ball and her late former husband, Desi Arnaz starred in the show from 1951 to 1957 [4] and it has since been widely syndicated in reruns. [5] Together they formed Desilu Productions [6] which made some of the top comedy shows of the '50s and '60s. [7] The couple divorced in 1960. [8] Following the divorce, Miss Ball married Gary Morton [9] and continued on in her successful show business career. [10] Her last appearance was at the 61st Academy Awards ceremony in March 1989.
