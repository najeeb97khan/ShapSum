[1] The merchant ship Ravenscraig rescued 23 crewmembers of the Star of Alexandria, [2] which sank in rough seas 400 miles southeast of Cape Cod. [3] Two sailors are still missing. [4] The British-registered ship was en route from Athens to New York with a load of cement [5] when it began taking on water in the rough seas. [6] The US Coast Guard responded to the mayday call [7] and sent a jet and a C-130 to the scene. [8] The C-130 was dropped a radio to the crew [9] and they were able to establish communications. [10] Four hours later, the Ravenscraig, en route to England, rescued them.
