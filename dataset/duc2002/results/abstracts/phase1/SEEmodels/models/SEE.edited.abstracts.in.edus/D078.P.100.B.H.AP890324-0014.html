[1] The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences tries to keep Oscar's sheen from being tarnished by commercialism, [2] even though it itself suggested limousines bringing celebrities to the awards have door-mounted coolers bearing the Coca Cola logo. [3] It criticized MGM-UA for touting "Rain Man" nominations before they were made.
